What is BigCommerce?

BigCommerce is a SaaS e-commerce platform that powers online stores for businesses of all sizes. Founded in 2009, it has grown to become one of the leading platforms for mid-market and enterprise brands looking to scale their online presence.

What sets BigCommerce apart is its "headless commerce" capability — the ability to separate the commerce backend from the frontend presentation layer. This gives brands unprecedented flexibility in how they present their store to customers.

Headless Commerce

BigCommerce's headless commerce capability is a major differentiator. Here's what it means:

Traditional E-commerce

The frontend (how the store looks) is tightly coupled with the backend (where data is stored and processed). Changes to one often require changes to the other.

Headless Commerce

BigCommerce provides the backend (product catalog, cart, checkout, payments) via API. You can use any frontend technology:

  • Custom React/Vue/Angular apps
  • Headless CMS (Contentful, Strapi)
  • Mobile apps
  • Kiosks and digital displays
  • Voice interfaces

Benefits of Headless

  • Flexibility: Customize every aspect of the customer experience
  • Performance: Build lightning-fast storefronts
  • Omnichannel: One backend, multiple frontends
  • Future-proof: Swap frontends without re-platforming

Enterprise Feature: BigCommerce's headless commerce is available on Standard and higher plans, making enterprise-grade architecture accessible to growing businesses.
