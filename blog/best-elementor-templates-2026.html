Template Kits vs Individual Templates

Elementor offers both individual templates and template kits:

Individual Templates

Perfect for adding specific pages to an existing website. Import just the pages you need without changing your current design.

Template Kits (Pro)

A complete website design including all essential pages:

  • Homepage
  • About page
  • Services/Products page
  • Contact page
  • Blog pages
  • Header and Footer designs

Pro Tip: Template kits are the fastest way to launch a complete website. Most kits include 10-20 pages covering every aspect of a professional website.

Where to Find Templates

  • Elementor Library: Free templates available directly in the editor
  • My Elementor: Free and premium templates from Elementor's marketplace
  • ThemeForest: Third-party templates and template kits
  • TemplateMonster: Premium templates with lifetime support
  • Envato Elements: Subscription-based access to thousands of templates

How to Import Templates

Step 1: Open the page where you want to add the template (or create a new page)

Step 2: Click the folder icon in the Elementor editor

Step 3: Browse or search for templates

Step 4: Click "Insert" to import the template

Step 5: Customize the template with your content

Tips for Using Templates

  • Don't Over-Customize: Templates are designed by experts - trust the layout
  • Replace Images: Always use your own photos instead of stock images
  • Update Colors: Adjust the color scheme to match your brand
  • Simplify Content: Remove sections you don't need
  • Mobile Preview: Check how your template looks on mobile devices
  • Test CTAs: Ensure all calls-to-action point to correct destinations
