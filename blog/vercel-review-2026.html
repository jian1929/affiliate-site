What is Vercel?

Vercel is the cloud platform built for frontend developers. Founded by the creators of Next.js, Vercel offers the best deployment experience for React, Next.js, and other modern frameworks. In 2026, Vercel hosts over 1 million projects and processes billions of requests daily.

Vercel's mission is to make frontend deployment as simple as git push. They handle everything else — CDN, edge functions, automatic SSL, and global caching.
