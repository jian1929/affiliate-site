What is Appointment Scheduling Software?

Appointment scheduling software allows businesses to accept bookings online without back-and-forth emails. It eliminates double-booking, sends automatic reminders, and syncs with your calendar.

Businesses using scheduling software see 30% fewer no-shows and save 8+ hours per week on appointment coordination.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which scheduling software has the best affiliate program?

Calendly offers one of the best programs at 25% recurring commission. Squarespace/Acuity offers 30% on first payment. Both are excellent choices.

Do I need a scheduling tool if I use Google Calendar?

Google Calendar alone doesn't let others book time with you. Scheduling software adds booking pages, automated reminders, and payment processing.

Can I use scheduling software for free consultations?

Yes! All major platforms allow free appointments. Calendly and Setmore have excellent free tiers for this use case.

How do reminders work?

All platforms send automatic email and/or SMS reminders before appointments. This reduces no-shows by up to 50%.
