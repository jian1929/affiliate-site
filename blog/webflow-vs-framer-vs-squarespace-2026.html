Framer Review

Framer is the modern choice for designers who want to create stunning, interactive websites without code. It's particularly popular among startups and design agencies.

✅ Pros

  • Incredibly smooth animations and interactions
  • Modern, sleek templates
  • Easy collaboration features
  • Quick to publish beautiful sites

❌ Cons

  • Limited e-commerce capabilities
  • Higher starting price at $25/month
  • Less flexible for complex web apps

Squarespace Review

Squarespace is the all-in-one solution for those who want beautiful websites without the complexity. It's perfect for photographers, artists, and small businesses.

✅ Pros

  • Easiest to use of the three
  • Beautiful award-winning templates
  • Integrated e-commerce and scheduling
  • Excellent customer support

❌ Cons

  • Less design flexibility
  • No code export capability
  • Limited third-party integrations

Which Should You Choose?

Choose Webflow if:

  • You need complete design control
  • You're building a complex website or web app
  • You want to export code for other platforms
  • You're a professional designer or developer

Choose Framer if:

  • You want the most modern, beautiful sites
  • You're a startup or design agency
  • Animations and interactions are priorities
  • You don't need extensive e-commerce

Choose Squarespace if:

  • Ease of use is your top priority
  • You're a photographer or creative professional
  • You want integrated e-commerce and scheduling
  • You prefer templates over custom designs
