Best Exit Intent Popup Tools 2026: Recover 15% More Conversions

Exit intent popups detect when a visitor is about to leave your website and display a targeted message at the critical moment. Used correctly, exit intent technology can recover 15% of abandoning visitors who would otherwise be lost forever.

This guide compares the top exit intent popup tools to help you choose the right solution for your conversion optimization strategy.

Understanding Exit Intent Technology

Exit intent technology tracks mouse movements to detect when visitors move their cursor toward the browser's close button or back button. At that precise moment, you can:

  • Display a special offer
  • Offer a discount code
  • Ask for email subscription
  • Show a compelling CTA
  • Display social proof

Key benefits:

  • Non-intrusive (only triggers when leaving)
  • Highly targeted messaging
  • Multiple use cases
  • Easy to implement
  • Measurable ROI

Exit Intent Best Practices

  1. Offer genuine value: A discount or free resource, not just a sales pitch
  2. Time it right: Don't trigger immediately; wait until they've engaged
  3. Make it easy to close: Frustrated users don't convert
  4. Test different offers: What works varies by audience
  5. Mobile considerations: Use slide-ins or bottom bars instead of popups
  6. Don't overdo it: One exit intent per session maximum
