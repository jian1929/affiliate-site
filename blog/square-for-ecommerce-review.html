Square for E-commerce: Overview

Start Accepting Payments Today

Square offers one of the easiest ways to accept payments online and in person. Get started with Square — free to set up, no monthly fees.

Square has evolved far beyond its original square card reader. Today, it's a comprehensive payment platform serving over 2 million businesses with solutions for POS, online payments, invoicing, and business management.

In this review, we analyze Square's e-commerce capabilities, pricing, integrations, and how it compares to competitors like Stripe and PayPal for online businesses.

Who Is Square Best For?

  • Omnichannel retailers (online + physical stores)
  • Small to medium businesses wanting simple setup
  • Food service businesses (restaurants, cafes, food trucks)
  • Service-based businesses (salons, contractors, consultants)
  • Businesses already using Square POS

Key Features for E-commerce

1. Square Online Store

Build a complete e-commerce website with Square's integrated builder:

  • Drag-and-drop website builder
  • 50+ professional templates
  • Built-in SEO tools
  • Blog functionality
  • Inventory management
  • Real-time carrier shipping rates
  • Item options (sizes, colors, variants)
  • Product categories and search

2. Square Online Checkout

Add buy buttons and checkout to any website:

  • Square Payment Form integration
  • PCI-compliant hosted fields
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay support
  • Saved payment methods
  • Automatic tax calculation

3. Square Invoices

Create professional invoices and get paid online:

  • Custom branded invoices
  • Automatic payment reminders
  • Accept credit card and ACH payments
  • Recurring billing
  • Time tracking integration

4. Square Virtual Terminal

Take payments through your computer:

  • Process cards manually (keyed-in)
  • No extra hardware needed
  • Instant receipt generation
  • Perfect for phone orders

5. Business Management Tools

  • Square POS: Unified inventory across channels
  • Square Appointments: Booking for service businesses
  • Square Payroll: Built-in payroll processing
  • Square Loyalty: Customer rewards program
  • Square Analytics: Sales reports and insights

Additional Fees

  • No monthly fee for basic Square Online
  • Square Online Plus: $29/month (includes domain, advanced features)
  • Square for Retail: Free to $60/month
  • Square for Restaurants: Free to $60/month
  • Chargeback fee: $15 per dispute
  • Refund processing: Free (Square returns the full processing fee)

Pro Tip: Square's pricing is simple — no tiers, no hidden fees. You pay the same rate regardless of volume. For high-volume businesses (>$250K/year), consider negotiating custom rates directly with Square.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • No monthly fees for basic online payments
  • Free e-commerce website included
  • Unified inventory across online and physical stores
  • Excellent POS integration for omnichannel
  • Instant deposits available (for extra fee)
  • Trusted brand with PCI-compliant infrastructure
  • No setup or cancellation fees
  • Built-in invoicing and virtual terminal

Cons

  • Higher rates than Stripe for card-not-present transactions
  • Limited customization compared to custom-built solutions
  • No built-in multi-currency (unlike Stripe)
  • Account freezes can happen without warning for high-risk industries
  • Slower international expansion compared to Stripe/PayPal

Our Verdict

When to Choose Square for E-commerce

Choose Square if:

  • You have (or plan to have) a physical store alongside your online shop
  • You want the simplest setup with no monthly fees
  • You're already using Square POS
  • You need unified inventory management
  • You want invoicing + online payments in one platform

When to Choose a Competitor

Choose Stripe if:

  • You need maximum customization
  • You're building a marketplace
  • You need multi-currency support
  • You're a developer who wants full API control

Choose PayPal if:

  • You want the trust of the PayPal brand
  • You need extensive international coverage
  • You're primarily a service business
