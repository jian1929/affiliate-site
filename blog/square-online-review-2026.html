What is Square Online?

Square, now known as Block (NYSE: SQ), started in 2009 with a simple mission: make commerce accessible to everyone. The company's first product was a small, square-shaped card reader that plugged into smartphone headphone jacks, democratizing credit card processing for small businesses.

Square Online (formerly Square Websites and earlier, Squareup) extends this mission to the web. It's a complete e-commerce platform that integrates seamlessly with Square's industry-leading point-of-sale system, making it ideal for businesses that operate both online and in physical locations.

Key Takeaway: Square Online is the best choice for businesses that need unified online and in-person commerce. Its tight POS integration, free tier, and straightforward pricing make it ideal for small businesses and growing retailers. If you're already using Square POS, Square Online is a natural extension.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Square Online really free?

Yes, Square Online's free plan is genuinely free with no time limit. You get unlimited products, standard themes, and full POS integration. You only pay transaction fees (2.9% + $0.30) when you make a sale. The paid plans add features and reduce transaction fees.

How does Square Online compare to Shopify?

Shopify offers more customization and a larger app ecosystem, making it better for complex e-commerce needs. Square Online excels for businesses with physical stores due to superior POS integration. For pure online stores without POS needs, Shopify may be more powerful. For omnichannel retail, Square Online is often the better choice.

Can I use Square Online without Square POS?

Absolutely. While Square Online pairs beautifully with Square POS, you can use it as a standalone e-commerce platform. Your transactions will process through Square's payment system, but you don't need physical POS hardware.

Does Square Online charge transaction fees on top of the monthly fee?

Yes, transaction fees apply on all plans. However, paid plan subscribers receive reduced rates: Plus (2.6% + $0.30) and Premium (2.5% + $0.30) compared to Free (2.9% + $0.30).

Can I sell on Instagram and Facebook with Square Online?

Yes, Square Online integrates directly with Instagram Shopping and Facebook Shop. You can sync your product catalog and enable social selling from the Square Online dashboard.

What happens to my store if I cancel Square?

If you cancel your Square Online store, you can export your product data and customer information. However, your Square Online URL will no longer be active. Make sure to export data before canceling.
