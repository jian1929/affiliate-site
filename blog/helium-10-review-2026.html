What is Helium 10?

Helium 10 is an all-in-one Amazon seller software suite that helps you find profitable products, research keywords, optimize listings, track competitors, and manage your business. It combines 10 powerful tools in one platform:

1. Black Box

Product research tool that lets you filter through millions of Amazon products to find high-demand, low-competition opportunities.

2. Cerebro

Reverse ASIN lookup tool that reveals the exact keywords any competitor is ranking for on Amazon.

3. Frankenstein

Keyword processor that cleans, filters, and organizes your keyword lists for optimal listing optimization.

4. Misspellinator

Finds common misspellings that customers make when searching, capturing keywords competitors miss.

5. Xray

Amazon product research extension that shows sales estimates, revenue data, and competitor analysis directly on Amazon.

6. Keyword Tracker

Monitor your keyword rankings over time and get alerts when your position changes.

7. Alerts

Track competitor price changes, stock levels, and review updates in real-time.

8. Inventory Protector

Protect your inventory from clearance sale hunters and prevent accidental overselling.

9. Follow-up

Automated email sequences to request product reviews from buyers.

10. Analytics

Comprehensive dashboard showing your sales trends, profit margins, and business health.
