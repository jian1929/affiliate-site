VoIP vs Traditional Phone Systems

Why Switch to VoIP?

  • Cost Savings: 50-70% less than traditional PBX systems
  • Flexibility: Work from anywhere with any device
  • Scalability: Add/remove users instantly
  • Features: Video, messaging, analytics included
  • Reliability: 99.999% uptime with cloud redundancy

What to Look for in VoIP Service

  • Uptime guarantee: Look for 99.9% or higher
  • Call quality: HD voice and noise cancellation
  • Integrations: CRM, helpdesk, productivity tools
  • Support: 24/7 availability and response time
  • International calling: Rates and coverage for global teams

Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need for VoIP?

Most VoIP services work with computers, smartphones, and existing devices. You can use softphones (software phones), USB headsets, or IP phones. Some providers offer free equipment with annual plans.

Is VoIP reliable for business?

Yes, modern VoIP services offer 99.999% uptime with redundant data centers. Quality is excellent with good internet connectivity. Features like automatic failover ensure you never miss calls.

Can I keep my existing phone number?

Yes, number porting is standard with all major VoIP providers. The process typically takes 5-10 business days.

How much does VoIP cost?

Prices range from $10-50 per user/month depending on features. Most providers offer per-user pricing with volume discounts. International calling is usually $0.01-0.05 per minute.
