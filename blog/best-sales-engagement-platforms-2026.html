  • Commission: 25% recurring (Outreach Affiliate Program)
  • Cookie Duration: 90 days
  • Pros: Industry leader, powerful AI, excellent analytics

2. Apollo.io

Starting at $39/user/month

Apollo combines sales engagement with a massive B2B database, making it a one-stop shop for prospecting and outreach.

  • Commission: 30% recurring
  • Cookie Duration: 60 days
  • Pros: Built-in data, affordable, all-in-one
  • Cons: Data accuracy varies, basic reporting

3. Salesloft

Starting at $80/user/month

Salesloft excels at cadence-based selling with strong email tracking and a user-friendly interface that sales reps love.

  • Commission: 25% recurring
  • Cookie Duration: 90 days
  • Pros: Easy to use, great mobile app
  • Cons: Limited customization, fewer integrations

4. HubSpot Sales Hub

Starting at $50/user/month

HubSpot Sales Hub integrates seamlessly with HubSpot CRM, offering email tracking, meeting scheduling, and calling within the HubSpot ecosystem.

  • Commission: 30% recurring
  • Cookie Duration: 180 days
  • Pros: Free CRM, HubSpot integration
  • Cons: Best with HubSpot CRM
