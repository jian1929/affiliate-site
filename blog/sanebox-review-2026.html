What is Sanebox?

Sanebox is an AI-powered email management system that learns your preferences and automatically filters, prioritizes, and archives emails you don't need to see immediately. It works with your existing email provider without requiring you to change anything.

Think of Sanebox as having a highly organized executive assistant who pre-sorts your inbox before you even open it. The AI learns which senders are important, which newsletters are worth reading, and which emails are noise.

Key Features

  • SaneBlackHole: Unsubscribe from emails with one click permanently
  • SaneNext: Remind yourself to follow up on emails later
  • SaneNoReplies: Track emails that haven't been replied to
  • SaneCC: Filter emails you're only CC'd on
  • SaneReminders: Auto-remind yourself about important emails
  • Smart Filters: AI learns and applies intelligent filters
  • Daily Digest: Summary of non-urgent emails
  • Multiple Folders: Automatically sorted into SaneBox, SaneNews, SaneBlackHole, and more

How It Works

Sanebox connects to your email and creates smart filters:

  • SaneBox: Emails from important contacts land here
  • SaneLater: Newsletters and non-urgent items
  • SaneNews: Curated newsletters you actually want
  • SaneBlackHole: Permanently blocked senders
  • SaneBulk: Mass marketing emails

Frequently Asked Questions

Sanebox uses AI to analyze email headers and patterns, not the content of messages. Your email content remains private.

What email providers work with Sanebox?

Sanebox works with Gmail, Google Workspace, Outlook, Office 365, iCloud, Yahoo, and most IMAP providers.

Can I override the AI decisions?

Yes, you can easily move emails between folders and Sanebox learns from your corrections.

How long does it take to learn my preferences?

Sanebox starts working immediately and typically learns your preferences within 1-2 weeks of regular use.
