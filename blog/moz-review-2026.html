What is Moz?

Moz was founded in 2001 by Rand Fishkin, making it one of the oldest SEO companies in existence. While others have copied its features, Moz continues to innovate with tools like Keyword Explorer, Link Research, and the famous Domain Authority metric that became an industry standard.

Key Features

  • Keyword Explorer: Find ranking opportunities with difficulty scores, SERP analysis, and volume data
  • Link Research: Discover backlink opportunities, analyze competitor links, and track new links
  • Domain Authority: The industry-standard metric for measuring website strength
  • Site Crawl: Technical SEO audits with actionable recommendations
  • Ranking Tracking: Monitor keyword positions across locations and devices
  • On-Page Optimization: Content suggestions and page-by-page analysis
  • Link Building Tools: Find outreach opportunities and manage campaigns

What Makes Moz Special

Moz pioneered several SEO concepts that others have adopted:

  • Page Authority: Similar metric for individual pages
  • Spam Score: Identify potentially harmful backlinks
  • MozBar: Browser extension for instant SEO metrics
  • Fresh Web Explorer: Monitor brand mentions and link opportunities
