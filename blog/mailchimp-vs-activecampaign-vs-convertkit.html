When to Choose Each Platform

Choose Mailchimp if:

  • You're just starting with email marketing
  • You need a generous free tier
  • You prefer visual design tools over automation power
  • You want the most recognizable brand

Choose ActiveCampaign if:

  • You need advanced marketing automation
  • You want CRM capabilities built-in
  • You're serious about customer experience automation
  • You want to earn affiliate income (30% recurring)
  • You're scaling past 1,000 subscribers

Choose ConvertKit if:

  • You're a content creator (blogger, podcaster, YouTuber)
  • You want simplicity over feature complexity
  • You focus on selling digital products or courses
  • You prefer clean, straightforward email tools

Automation Capabilities

Automation is where the platforms diverge most significantly:

Mailchimp

Offers basic automation like welcome emails and abandoned cart reminders. Good for simple sequences but limited for complex customer journeys.

ActiveCampaign

Industry-leading automation with visual workflow builder, conditional branching, and CRM integration. You can automate entire customer lifecycles.

ConvertKit

Visual automation with a focus on creator needs. Great for email courses, content upgrades, and creator-focused funnels.
