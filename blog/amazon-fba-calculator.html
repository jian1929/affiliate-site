Profit Calculation Formula

How to Improve Your Margins

1. Negotiate Product Costs

Even a 10% reduction in product cost can significantly boost margins. Build relationships with suppliers and order larger quantities.

2. Optimize Shipping

Compare LTL vs. parcel shipping. For heavier products, LTL can be significantly cheaper.

3. Reduce Weight

Lighter products have lower fulfillment fees. Consider packaging redesign to reduce weight and size.

4. Adjust Pricing

Small price increases have outsized impact on profit. A 5% price increase on 25% margins = 20% profit increase.

5. Focus on High-Margin Products

Use tools like Helium 10 or Jungle Scout to find products with inherently higher margins.
