Systeme.io: The Budget-Friendly All-in-One

Best for: New creators, budget-conscious marketers, and anyone wanting the lowest barrier to entry.

Systeme.io has disrupted the online course market by offering a genuinely free plan with unlimited students, emails, and blog posts. Their affiliate program pays an industry-leading 60% recurring commission with a massive 180-day cookie duration—the longest in the industry.

The platform includes:

  • Course and funnel builder
  • Unlimited email marketing
  • Sales funnels and landing pages
  • Affiliate management system
  • Blog with SEO tools

Commission structure: 60% recurring lifetime commission on all plans

Kajabi: The Premium Powerhouse

Best for: Established creators with established audiences and higher budgets.

Kajabi is the premium choice, offering sophisticated automation, beautiful templates, and a polished brand experience. However, this comes at a price—starting at $149/month.

Their affiliate program offers 30% recurring commissions with a 30-day cookie. While lower than Systeme.io, Kajabi affiliates often convert higher-ticket sales due to the platform's premium positioning.

Teachable: The Flexible Middle Ground

Best for: Course creators who prioritize teaching over marketing.

Teachable offers a free plan (with transaction fees) and paid plans starting at $29/month. While it lacks built-in sales funnels, it excels at course delivery and student management.

Teachable's affiliate program pays 30% recurring commissions with a 30-day cookie.
