Live vs. Evergreen Webinars

One of the biggest decisions you'll make is between live-only and evergreen webinar platforms:

Live Webinars

Traditional live webinars where you present in real-time. Best for:

  • Building immediate urgency and FOMO
  • Live Q&A sessions
  • Product launches with exclusivity
  • Community building and engagement

Evergreen Webinars

Automated webinars that play on-demand 24/7. Best for:

  • Generating leads around the clock
  • Serving international audiences
  • Consistent sales without live effort
  • Onboarding and training

Which Type Do You Need?

Go with Live: If you want to build a community, sell high-ticket items with urgency, or need live interaction.
Go with Evergreen: If you want to generate sales 24/7 and reach audiences across time zones.

Maximize Your Webinar Success

Pre-Webinar Checklist

  • Create compelling registration page with clear value proposition
  • Set up automated email reminders (24hr, 1hr, 15min before)
  • Test all technology 30 minutes before going live
  • Prepare slides, demos, and backup content
  • Have a co-host for chat moderation

During the Webinar

  • Start 2-3 minutes early to greet attendees
  • Use polls every 5-7 minutes to maintain engagement
  • Answer questions from chat in real-time
  • Include at least 2 clear calls-to-action
  • Keep energy high and be enthusiastic

Post-Webinar Optimization

  • Send follow-up emails within 24 hours
  • Share recording with registrants who missed
  • Review analytics to improve future webinars
  • Nurture leads with a follow-up sequence
  • Repurpose content into blog posts and social media

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does webinar software cost?

Webinar software ranges from $40/month (Zoom Webinar) to $500+/month for enterprise plans. Most platforms offer tiered pricing based on features and attendee limits.

What's the best webinar platform for beginners?

Demio is excellent for beginners due to its intuitive interface and built-in marketing tools. Zoom Webinar is also beginner-friendly if you already use Zoom for meetings.

Can I record my webinars?

Yes, all major webinar platforms include recording capabilities. Cloud recording is typically included, with local recording available on most platforms.

What's an evergreen webinar?

An evergreen webinar is a pre-recorded presentation that plays automatically on-demand. This allows you to generate leads and sales 24/7 without being live. WebinarJam and Demio excel at evergreen functionality.

How many attendees can join a webinar?

Maximum attendees vary by platform: Zoom (10,000), WebinarJam (5,000), GoToWebinar (3,000), and Demio (1,500). Most businesses don't need the maximum capacity.

Do I need special equipment for webinars?

A good webcam (even your laptop camera), quality microphone, and stable internet connection are essential. Consider a ring light and external microphone for professional production quality.

Which platform has the best affiliate program?

WebinarJam offers the highest commission at 40-50% recurring. Demio and GoToWebinar offer solid 25-30% recurring commissions. Zoom offers 15% per sale.
