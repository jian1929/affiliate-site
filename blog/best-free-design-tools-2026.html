Detailed Reviews

Canva (Best Overall)

Canva dominates the free design space with over 250,000 templates, brand kit features, and magic resize. The free tier includes access to most features, making it perfect for social media managers and small businesses.

Figma (Best for Teams)

Figma revolutionized collaborative design. The free tier includes 3 active projects and unlimited viewers, making it ideal for small design teams starting out.

Penpot (Best Open Source)

Penpot is the first open-source design and prototyping tool. Browser-based with no downloads required, it's a viable free alternative to Figma.
