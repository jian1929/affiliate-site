Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best SMS marketing platform for e-commerce?

Klaviyo is our top recommendation for e-commerce brands. It offers the best combination of email + SMS integration, powerful automation, native e-commerce connections, and competitive pricing. Brands report average ROI of $40+ per SMS campaign.

How much does SMS marketing cost?

SMS marketing costs vary by platform. Most charge $15-$50/month base fee plus $0.02-$0.05 per message sent. Some platforms like SMSBump offer free plans with limited usage. For a store with 5,000 subscribers sending 4 campaigns/month, expect to pay $100-$300/month total.

Is Klaviyo worth the price?

Yes, for e-commerce brands. Klaviyo's unified email + SMS platform saves time, improves customer data consistency, and typically delivers 5-10x higher engagement than email alone. The built-in revenue attribution shows clear ROI, and brands using Klaviyo report strong returns that justify the investment.

What's the difference between Twilio and SMS marketing platforms?

Twilio is SMS infrastructure (APIs and building blocks), not a marketing platform. You build everything yourself - opt-in forms, campaigns, automation. Dedicated SMS platforms like Klaviyo offer pre-built flows, templates, and features designed for marketers, not developers. Choose Twilio only if you need complete customization and have development resources.

Can I use SMS marketing without a large subscriber list?

Absolutely. SMSBump offers a free plan for up to 250 contacts, and Klaviyo's free plan includes 250 contacts. Even with a small list, SMS delivers exceptional engagement. Start building your SMS list today - every opt-in is valuable.

What is a good SMS opt-in rate for e-commerce?

Average SMS opt-in rates range from 3-8% of website visitors using standard pop-ups. Brands using creative incentives (discount codes, free shipping) see opt-in rates of 10-15%. Exit-intent pop-ups and checkout opt-ins typically perform best.
