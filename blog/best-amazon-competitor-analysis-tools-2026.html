Getting Started with Competitor Analysis

No matter which tool you choose, here's a basic process to get started:

  1. Identify 5-10 top competitors in your category
  2. Run reverse ASIN to see their top keywords
  3. Track their pricing over 2-4 weeks
  4. Analyze their listings: images, titles, bullet points
  5. Monitor their reviews: what do customers love/hate?
  6. Track their rank for your target keywords
  7. Set up alerts for significant changes
