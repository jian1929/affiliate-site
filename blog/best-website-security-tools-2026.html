Frequently Asked Questions

Do I really need website security?

Yes, absolutely. Over 30,000 websites are hacked daily, and small business sites are often targets because hackers assume they have weak security. A single breach can cost thousands in recovery, destroy your search rankings, and permanently damage your reputation. Prevention is always cheaper than cure.

What's the difference between a CDN and security service?

A CDN (Content Delivery Network) like Cloudflare speeds up your site by caching content globally. Security services like Sucuri focus on malware detection, removal, and WAF protection. Many services combine both: Cloudflare offers basic security, while Sucuri offers malware-specific protection.

Can I use multiple security tools together?

Yes, but be careful about conflicts. Wordfence + Cloudflare works well. Sucuri + Cloudflare works but you may need to configure settings to avoid conflicts. Avoid running two WAFs simultaneously as they can block legitimate traffic.

What should I do if my site is already hacked?

Stop panicking and act quickly: 1) Take your site offline or add a maintenance mode, 2) Change all passwords, 3) Contact Sucuri or a malware removal service, 4) Restore from a clean backup if available, 5) Audit all user accounts, 6) Update all software. Sucuri offers emergency malware removal services.
