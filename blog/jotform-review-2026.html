What is JotForm?

JotForm is one of the longest-standing and most feature-rich form builders in the market. Founded in 2006, this San Francisco-based company has evolved from a simple form creation tool into a comprehensive platform that serves millions of users worldwide, from freelancers and small businesses to Fortune 500 companies.

What sets JotForm apart is its remarkable balance of power and accessibility. The platform offers an impressive array of form types, including traditional web forms, PDF forms, checkout forms, and even printable documents. Unlike newer form builders that focus on aesthetics, JotForm has built its reputation on functionality and flexibility.

JotForm has also pioneered several industry firsts, including the ability to create fillable PDF forms and generate documents from form submissions. This document generation capability has made it particularly popular among businesses that need to automate paperwork workflows.

Key Takeaway: JotForm is the workhorse of form builders—less flashy than Typeform but arguably more powerful. It offers more form types, better document automation, and a pricing structure that scales better for high-volume users.

You can design PDF forms with the same drag-and-drop interface, collect PDF signatures, and automatically generate completed PDFs from submissions. Generate Word documents, PDFs, and contracts automatically from form submissions. Set up templates with placeholders, and JotForm fills them in with submitted data. Perfect for contracts, invoices, proposals, and any document that needs to be personalized.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is JotForm better than Typeform?

It depends on your needs. JotForm is better for complex forms, document automation, PDF generation, and value at higher volumes. Typeform is better for conversational forms, beautiful presentations, and user experience. If you need power and flexibility, JotForm wins. If you need beautiful simplicity, Typeform is the choice.

Can I create fillable PDFs with JotForm?

Yes, this is one of JotForm's standout features. You can create PDFs with fillable form fields, collect e-signatures, and automatically generate completed PDF documents from submissions. This is invaluable for businesses that need to create contracts, applications, or any printable documents.

Does JotForm have a free trial for paid plans?

JotForm offers a free tier that lets you explore basic functionality. For paid features, you can upgrade and cancel within 5 days for a full refund—no questions asked.

Can I accept payments through JotForm?

Absolutely. JotForm integrates with over 30 payment processors including Stripe, PayPal, Square, Authorize.net, and Braintree. You can create order forms, donation forms, subscription forms, and more without any coding.

Is JotForm HIPAA compliant?

Yes, JotForm offers HIPAA-compliant form building on their Enterprise plan. This includes Business Associate Agreements (BAAs), encrypted data storage, and specialized form templates designed for healthcare organizations.
