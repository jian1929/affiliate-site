Pricing Plans (2026)

Unbounce offers three main pricing tiers designed to accommodate businesses of various sizes, from startups to enterprise organizations.

Plan Price Key Features
Starter $99/month Up to 5 domains, 75 landing pages, 20,000 visitors
Optimization $199/month Up to 3 domains, unlimited pages, 100,000 visitors, A/B testing
Conversion $399/month Unlimited domains, unlimited pages, 300,000 visitors, all features

Prices shown are for annual billing. Monthly billing is available at higher rates. Enterprise pricing is available upon request.

What's Included at Each Tier

Starter ($99/mo): Perfect for small businesses and freelancers getting started with landing pages. Includes basic templates, popup builder, and standard integrations. Limited to 5 connected domains and 20,000 monthly visitors.

Optimization ($199/mo): The most popular tier for growing businesses. Adds A/B testing, advanced analytics, AI-powered copywriting suggestions, and significantly higher traffic limits. Includes sticky bars and overlays.

Conversion ($399/mo): The full Unbounce experience with unlimited domains, maximum traffic allowances, advanced personalization, client billing, and priority support. Includes all apps and integrations.

Enterprise Options

For large organizations, Unbounce offers custom enterprise pricing with features including dedicated account management, custom integrations, advanced security features, and volume discounts. Contact their sales team for a customized quote.

Key Features

Drag-and-Drop Builder

Unbounce's builder is renowned for its intuitive interface. The drag-and-drop editor allows you to easily add, move, and customize elements without any coding knowledge. Key builder features include:

  • Sticky Boxes: Lock important elements in place as visitors scroll
  • Section Cloning: Quickly duplicate sections across pages
  • Responsive Preview: Test how pages look on desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • Undo/Redo: Experiment freely knowing you can always go back
  • Auto-save: Never lose your work with automatic saving

Conversion Intelligence (AI-Powered)

Unbounce's AI features analyze visitor behavior and provide recommendations to improve conversion rates. The system can suggest optimal button colors, headline variations, and form field arrangements based on millions of data points from similar campaigns.

A/B Testing & Conversion Benchmarking

Unbounce makes A/B testing accessible and actionable. The platform provides:

  • Visual A/B Editor: Create variations by simply clicking and editing
  • Statistical Significance Calculator: Know when results are valid
  • Traffic Splitting: Automatically route visitors between variations
  • Winner Detection: The system highlights statistically significant winners
  • Conversion Benchmarking: Compare your pages against industry averages

Template Library

Unbounce offers over 100 professionally designed templates, each optimized for specific use cases:

  • Lead generation pages
  • Webinar registration
  • Free trial signups
  • Product launches
  • Download pages (whitepapers, ebooks)
  • Contact forms
  • Appointment scheduling
  • E-commerce product pages

Popup Builder & Sticky Bars

Beyond landing pages, Unbounce includes tools for creating:

  • Popup Boxes: Timed or trigger-based popups for special offers
  • Sticky Bars: Fixed header bars for announcements
  • Slide-Ins: Non-intrusive notifications that slide in from corners
  • Smart Blocks: AI-optimized CTAs that adapt to visitor behavior

Integrations

Unbounce connects seamlessly with your existing marketing stack:

  • Email Marketing: Mailchimp, HubSpot, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, Klaviyo
  • CRM: Salesforce, HubSpot CRM, Pipedrive
  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Facebook Pixel
  • Other: Zapier, WordPress, Shopify, Stripe

AMP Landing Pages

For mobile-first campaigns, Unbounce offers Accelerated Mobile Pages (AMP) that load nearly instantly on mobile devices, potentially improving your Google Quality Score for mobile search ads.

Final Verdict

Is Unbounce Right for You?

Unbounce remains one of the most powerful landing page platforms available in 2026. Its combination of conversion-focused features, robust A/B testing, and AI-powered optimization tools make it an excellent choice for marketers who are serious about maximizing their conversion rates. The platform's reliability and professional templates also make it a favorite among agencies managing multiple clients.

Choose Unbounce if:

  • Conversion optimization is your top priority
  • You run PPC campaigns and need dedicated landing pages
  • You need robust A/B testing capabilities
  • You want access to AI-powered optimization suggestions
  • You manage multiple clients or campaigns
  • You value reliability and professional templates
  • Popup and sticky bar functionality is important

Consider alternatives if:

  • You're on a tight budget
  • You need built-in email marketing
  • You want multi-user real-time collaboration
  • You need advanced e-commerce functionality
  • You prefer an all-in-one marketing platform
