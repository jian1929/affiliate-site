What is Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is a retail fulfillment method where you don't keep products in stock. Instead, when a customer orders from your store, you purchase the item from a third-party supplier who ships it directly to the customer.

You earn the difference between the supplier's price and your selling price.

Pros

  • Low startup cost ($29/month)
  • No inventory management
  • No warehousing or shipping
  • Work from anywhere
  • Easy to scale
  • Test products easily

Cons

  • Lower profit margins
  • No control over inventory
  • Shipping times can be long
  • Supplier errors affect you
  • High competition
  • Returns can be complex

Best Dropshipping Suppliers

DSers (Recommended)

Official Shopify partner. Direct integration with AliExpress. Offers US suppliers and faster shipping options.

Spocket

US and EU suppliers with 2-5 day shipping. Higher quality products but pricier wholesale.

AutoDS

Multi-platform tool connecting to eBay, Amazon, Walmart, and AliExpress suppliers.

AliExpress (Direct)

Cheapest source but longer shipping (7-21 days). Best for high-volume, low-margin products.
