Frequently Asked Questions

Do I really need antivirus software in 2026?

Absolutely. Cyber threats are more sophisticated than ever. Even if you're careful, malware can spread through email attachments, malicious websites, and USB drives. Quality antivirus software provides essential protection against ransomware, spyware, and other threats.

Is Windows Defender enough?

Windows Defender has improved significantly and provides decent baseline protection. However, premium antivirus software offers better detection rates, more advanced features, identity theft protection, and comprehensive customer support. For most users, premium antivirus is worth the investment.

Will antivirus slow down my computer?

Modern antivirus software is designed to have minimal impact on performance. Bitdefender and Kaspersky are particularly lightweight. Heavy impact typically occurs during full system scans, which you can schedule during off-hours.

Can I use free antivirus?

Free antivirus like Avast One Essential provides basic protection against common threats. However, premium versions offer significantly better protection, unlimited VPN, identity monitoring, and advanced features. For comprehensive protection, premium is recommended.

How do affiliate links work for antivirus?

When you purchase antivirus software through our links, we earn a commission (typically 20-40% of the sale). This comes at no extra cost to you and helps support our research. We only recommend antivirus software we believe in.

What's the difference between antivirus and internet security suites?

Antivirus software focuses on malware detection and removal. Internet security suites add features like firewall, spam filtering, parental controls, and sometimes VPN. Total security suites include all features plus identity theft protection and encrypted storage.
