SaneBox: The AI Email Assistant

SaneBox - AI-Powered Email Sorting

Commission: 30% lifetime commission

Cookie: 90 days

Why it wins: SaneBox uses AI to automatically sort your emails into folders, unsubscribe from newsletters you never read, and prioritize what matters. Set it up once and forget it.

Key Features

  • SaneLater: Automatic folder for newsletters and less important emails
  • SaneBlackHole: Unsubscribe from emails with one click, permanently
  • SaneNews: Dedicated folder for newsletters, read on your schedule
  • SaneAttachments: Notification when large attachments are coming
  • SaneDoNotDisturb: Silence notifications during focus time

How It Works

SaneBox analyzes your email patterns and automatically creates folders. When you receive an email:

  1. Important emails go to your inbox as normal
  2. Less important emails go to SaneLater
  3. Newsletters go to SaneNews
  4. Notifications go to SaneDigest

Pros

  • Works with any email client (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.)
  • Set it and forget it - AI does the work
  • Excellent 30% lifetime affiliate commission
  • 30-day money-back guarantee
  • Doesn't read your emails - just analyzes metadata

Cons

  • Requires granting folder access
  • No dedicated app - web dashboard only
  • Learning curve for folder organization

Superhuman: The Premium Experience

Superhuman - The Fastest Email Experience

What it offers: A premium email client built for speed. Superhuman claims you can process emails 3x faster using their keyboard-first interface.

Key Features

  • Split Inbox: See emails from specific senders prominently
  • Snippets: Save and insert templated responses instantly
  • Read Receipts: Know when your emails are opened
  • Scheduled Send: Send emails at the perfect time
  • Undo Send: 10-second window to recall emails
  • Calendar Integration: Schedule meetings without leaving email

The Superhuman Experience

Superhuman is designed around keyboard shortcuts. Every action is one or two keystrokes away:

  • J/K - Navigate between emails
  • E - Archive
  • Cmd+Enter - Send
  • Cmd+Shift+S - Schedule send

Pros

  • Fastest email processing with keyboard shortcuts
  • Beautiful, minimalist interface
  • Excellent for power users
  • Built-in calendar scheduling

Cons

  • Most expensive option at $30/month
  • Requires invite code to join
  • Only works with Gmail, Outlook, and iCloud
  • No built-in spam protection

Spam Hero: The Spam Fighter

Spam Hero - AI-Powered Spam Protection

What it offers: Simple, effective spam protection using machine learning. Spam Hero learns from your preferences to block what you don't want.

Key Features

  • AI Learning: Learns what you consider spam
  • Whitelist/Blacklist: Fine-tune your protection
  • Domain Blocking: Block all emails from specific domains
  • Mailbox Wizard: Auto-configure based on your provider

How It Works

Spam Hero works at the email server level:

Pros

  • AI improves over time
  • Affordable starting at $2.99/month

Cons

  • Requires DNS configuration
  • Learning curve for setup

Frequently Asked Questions

Does SaneBox read my emails?

No. SaneBox only analyzes email metadata (sender, subject, time) to make sorting decisions. It never reads the content of your emails.

Is Superhuman worth the price?

If you spend significant time on email and value speed, Superhuman can be worth it. The keyboard shortcuts genuinely speed up email processing. However, at $30/month, it's a premium choice.

Can I use multiple tools together?

Yes. Many users combine tools: Spam Hero for spam protection, SaneBox for sorting, and Clean Email for occasional cleanup.

Do these tools work with my email provider?

SaneBox works with Gmail, Outlook, iCloud, Yahoo, and most IMAP providers. Superhuman works with Gmail, Outlook, and iCloud. Clean Email and Spam Hero work with any email provider.
