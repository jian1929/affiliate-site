Common Questions

Can I use my own domain with Podia?

Yes! Podia supports custom domains on all paid plans. Point your domain to your Podia school for a fully branded experience.

How does the affiliate program work?

When someone clicks your affiliate link and signs up for Podia, you earn 30% of their monthly payment for life. If they upgrade, you earn more. If they stay for years, you earn for years.

Can I offer refunds on Podia?

Podia doesn't have built-in refunds, but you can set your own refund policy and handle it manually or through your payment processor.

Does Podia have a free trial?

Podia doesn't offer a free trial, but you can start on the Mover plan and upgrade or cancel anytime.
