Pricing Plans (2026)

Hootsuite offers tiered pricing designed for organizations of all sizes, from small teams to large enterprises.

Plan Price Best For
Professional $99/month (1 user) Small teams, solopreneurs
Team $249/month (up to 5 users) Growing teams needing collaboration
Business $599/month (up to 10 users) Mid-size businesses
Enterprise Custom pricing Large organizations, agencies

All plans include unlimited scheduled posts. Additional user seats can be added to Team and Business plans.

30% Recurring Affiliate Commission

Hootsuite offers a 30% recurring commission on all referred plans for the first 12 months. Enterprise referrals can earn significantly more with custom commission structures. The substantial price points make Hootsuite one of the highest-potential affiliate programs in the social media management space.

What's Included at Each Tier

Professional ($99/mo): 1 user, unlimited scheduled posts, analytics (up to 6 months), 1 social profile per platform, basic scheduling, mobile apps.

Team ($249/mo): Up to 5 users, everything in Professional, 5 profiles per platform, content calendar, team assignments, approval workflows, Hootsuite Ads.

Business ($599/mo): Up to 10 users, everything in Team, unlimited profiles, advanced analytics, custom reporting, API access, dedicated support, social listening.

Enterprise: Custom everything, unlimited users, white-label options, advanced security, custom integrations, dedicated account manager, SLA guarantees.

Final Verdict

Is Hootsuite Right for You?

Hootsuite remains the enterprise standard for social media management. Its comprehensive feature set, scalability, and proven enterprise capabilities make it the go-to choice for large organizations. However, the pricing and complexity mean it's not the right fit for everyone. For affiliates, Hootsuite's 30% commission and high price point offer substantial earning potential.

Choose Hootsuite if:

  • You're managing a large team (10+ people)
  • You need enterprise security and compliance
  • You manage multiple brands or clients
  • Social ROI reporting is critical
  • You need social listening capabilities
  • Enterprise integrations are essential
  • You want proven reliability at scale
  • You're targeting enterprise customers as an affiliate

Consider alternatives if:

  • You're a small team or solopreneur
  • Budget is a primary constraint
  • You prefer modern, intuitive interfaces
  • You only need basic scheduling
  • You want the best value per feature

Hootsuite has earned its position as the enterprise leader through years of consistent innovation and reliability. For large organizations and agencies, it remains the safe, proven choice. The platform continues to evolve with AI features and improved analytics, maintaining its relevance in an increasingly competitive market.
