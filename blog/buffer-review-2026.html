Pricing Plans (2026)

Buffer is known for its transparent, straightforward pricing with a generous free tier.

Plan Price Best For
Free Free (3 channels) Individuals getting started
Starter $6/channel/month Solopreneurs, small teams
Essentials $12/channel/month Growing businesses
Team $18/channel/month Teams needing collaboration
Agency Custom pricing Managing multiple clients

Prices shown per channel per month when billed annually. A channel is one social media account.

What's Included at Each Tier

Free: 3 social accounts, 10 scheduled posts per account, 1 user, basic analytics, 1 week analytics history, browser extension.

Starter ($6/channel/mo): Everything in Free plus unlimited scheduled posts, 2 weeks analytics history, first 3 channels free (then $6 each), landing page builder.

Essentials ($12/channel/mo): Everything in Starter plus 1 year analytics history, best time to post suggestions, hashtag suggestions, Pablo image creator, unlimited team members.

Team ($18/channel/mo): Everything in Essentials plus content calendar, approval workflow, team performance reports, client billing, 2 years analytics history.

Agency: Everything in Team plus white-label reports, client dashboard access, dedicated account manager, custom integrations.

30% Recurring Affiliate Commission

Buffer's affiliate program offers an impressive 30% recurring commission for the lifetime of referred customers. This means for every user you refer who pays $12/month, you earn $3.60/month for as long as they remain a customer. High-value customers on Team or Agency plans can generate substantial passive income.

Final Verdict

Is Buffer Right for You?

Buffer excels at doing what it promises: making social media management simple and effective. It's the perfect tool for content creators, solopreneurs, and small teams who want professional results without enterprise complexity. The platform's 30% recurring affiliate commission makes it particularly attractive for affiliate marketers.

Choose Buffer if:

  • You're a solopreneur or small team (1-10 people)
  • You value simplicity and ease of use
  • You want transparent, predictable pricing
  • You're building a long-term affiliate revenue stream
  • You prefer clean interfaces over feature overload
  • You need reliable scheduling without complexity
  • You want built-in image creation tools

Consider alternatives if:

  • You need advanced analytics and reporting
  • Social listening/monitoring is essential
  • You manage multiple client accounts (consider Buffer Agency)
  • You need deep Instagram engagement tools
  • Enterprise-level features are required
  • You need native social CRM capabilities

Buffer remains one of the most beloved social media management tools in the market. Its commitment to simplicity, transparency, and user experience continues to differentiate it from competitors. For those entering the social media management space or looking for a streamlined alternative to complex enterprise tools, Buffer delivers exactly what it promises.
