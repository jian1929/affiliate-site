Adyen: Overview

Get Started with Adyen

Adyen powers payments for some of the world's largest brands including Uber, Spotify, Airbnb, and Microsoft. Contact Adyen for enterprise payment solutions.

Adyen is a Dutch payment technology company that provides merchants with a single platform to accept payments online, in-app, and in-store across 135+ countries. Founded in 2006, Adyen went public in 2018 and has grown to become the payment processor of choice for enterprise brands seeking global scale.

Unlike many US-centric payment processors, Adyen was built from the ground up for international commerce, making it the top choice for brands expanding beyond their home markets.

Who Is Adyen Best For?

  • Large enterprises with global operations
  • Brands expanding internationally
  • Omnichannel retailers with online and physical stores
  • Marketplaces requiring sophisticated split payments
  • Companies processing high transaction volumes
  • Businesses requiring unified commerce insights

Pricing Structure

Adyen uses an interchange-plus pricing model, which is more transparent than flat-rate pricing:

Volume Discounts

Adyen offers significant discounts for high-volume merchants:

  • Volume-based interchange reduction
