Canva: The Design Powerhouse

Canva - All-in-One Design Platform

What it offers: Beyond presentations, Canva provides a complete design suite including social media graphics, videos, documents, and more.

Key Features

  • Magic Write: AI-powered text generation within presentations
  • 1000+ Templates: Massive library of professional designs
  • Brand Kit: Save and apply brand colors, fonts, and logos
  • Team Collaboration: Real-time editing with comments and approvals

Pros

Cons

Pros

Google Slides: The Free Option

Google Slides - Free and Collaborative

What it offers: A free, browser-based presentation tool that excels at collaboration.

Pros

Cons

  • Requires internet for full functionality
