What is EverWebinar?

EverWebinar is a specialized platform for creating and running fully automated webinars. Unlike traditional webinar tools where you need to be live, EverWebinar lets you pre-record your presentation and have it run automatically, creating the illusion of a live event for attendees.

This makes it ideal for:

Key Features

1. True Evergreen Automation

Record your webinar once, and let it run forever. EverWebinar creates a seamless experience where attendees believe they're joining a live event, complete with:

  • Simulated live chat from fake attendees
  • Real attendee Q&A and polls
  • Dynamic "live" countdown timers
  • Scheduled webinar moments throughout the day

2. JustCall Integration

Connect with your sales team in real-time. When an attendee raises their hand or wants to talk, the system can automatically connect them with a live sales rep via JustCall.

3. Dynamic Registrants

Personalize the webinar experience based on:

  • Source of registration (which page they came from)
  • Timezone (show relevant scheduling)
  • Previous interactions with your brand

4. Built-in Scheduling Engine

Let registrants choose their preferred time from multiple webinar showings. EverWebinar automatically assigns them to the next available session.

5. Lead Capture Forms

Collect information at key moments:

  • Pre-webinar registration
  • Mid-webinar checkpoints
  • Post-webinar follow-up offers
