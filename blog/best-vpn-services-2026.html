Frequently Asked Questions

Is using a VPN legal?

VPNs are legal in most countries including the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and EU member states. However, using VPNs for illegal activities remains illegal. Some countries including China, Russia, Iran, and UAE restrict or ban VPN usage.

Do VPNs slow down internet speed?

Quality VPNs may slightly reduce speeds due to encryption overhead, but premium services like ExpressVPN and NordVPN use optimized protocols that minimize this impact. In some cases, VPNs can improve speeds if your ISP throttles certain activities.

Can VPNs be detected and blocked?

Advanced VPNs like Surfshark (Camouflage Mode) and NordVPN (Obfuscated Servers) can bypass VPN detection. Free VPNs are more easily blocked. Netflix and other streaming services also actively block known VPN IP addresses.

Should I use free VPNs?

Free VPNs are not recommended due to privacy risks, data caps, speed limitations, and aggressive advertising. Some free VPNs have been caught selling user data. Premium VPNs offer better security and performance for a reasonable cost.

How do VPN affiliate programs work?

When you purchase a VPN through our links, we earn a commission (typically 30-40% for first year of subscription). This doesn't increase your price and helps support our research. We only recommend VPNs we've verified and believe in.

Which VPN is best for torrenting?

All four recommended VPNs support P2P/torrenting. NordVPN and CyberGhost have dedicated P2P-optimized servers. ExpressVPN and Surfshark allow torrenting on all servers. All have kill switches to protect your privacy if connections drop.

Do VPNs work on all devices?

Premium VPNs offer apps for Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, routers, smart TVs, and browsers. All recommendations support at least 6 simultaneous connections with Surfshark offering unlimited devices.

What's the difference between VPN protocols?

OpenVPN is open-source and widely supported. WireGuard is newer, faster, and more efficient. IKEv2 is stable for mobile. Proprietary protocols like Lightway (ExpressVPN) and NordLynx (NordVPN) are optimized for performance.

Can I get a dedicated IP with my VPN?

ExpressVPN doesn't offer dedicated IPs. NordVPN offers dedicated IPs for an additional fee. Surfshark offers static IPs in some locations. CyberGhost offers dedicated IPs in select countries.

What is a no-logs policy?

A no-logs policy means the VPN provider doesn't record your browsing activity, connection times, IP addresses, or any data that could identify you. ExpressVPN, NordVPN, Surfshark, and CyberGhost all claim strict no-logs policies and have been independently audited.
