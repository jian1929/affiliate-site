Building your first sales funnel sounds complicated. It doesn't have to be.

In this Systeme.io tutorial, I'll walk you through building a lead generation funnel from scratch. No technical skills. No credit card. Just 30 minutes of your time.

What You'll Build Today

  • A landing page (opt-in form)
  • An automated email sequence
  • A thank you page
  • Basic analytics tracking

By the end, you'll have a fully working funnel collecting leads on autopilot.

Step 1: Create Your Free Account

Go to systeme.io and click "Get Started Free."

Enter your email and create a password. That's it. No credit card.

Step 2: Choose a Funnel Template

Systeme.io gives you 100+ pre-built funnel templates. For your first funnel, choose:

  1. Click "Funnels" in the sidebar
  2. Click "Add New Funnel"
  3. Select "Lead Generation" category
  4. Pick a template you like
  5. Give your funnel a name

Step 3: Customize Your Landing Page

The template comes ready to go, but you'll want to:

  • Change the headline - Make it about your lead magnet
  • Update the description - What will they learn?
  • Add your lead magnet - Ebook, checklist, template, etc.
  • Set up your form - Name and email (keep it simple)

Pro tip: Keep your form to just 2 fields (name + email). More fields = fewer signups.

Step 4: Create Your Email Sequence

This is where the magic happens. Go to "Emails" → "Automations":

  1. Click "Create Automation"
  2. Set the trigger: "New subscriber"
  3. Add your welcome email (send immediately)
  4. Add follow-up emails (days 2, 4, 7)
  5. Connect each email to your funnel

Your emails should:

  • Introduce yourself
  • Deliver value (your lead magnet)
  • Hint at your paid offer
  • Have one clear call-to-action

Step 5: Set Up the Thank You Page

The thank you page is often forgotten but crucial:

  • Confirm what they signed up for
  • Tell them when to expect the email
  • Hint at what you'll offer next
  • Keep them engaged while waiting

Step 6: Test Everything

Before driving traffic:

  1. Go through your funnel as a test subscriber
  2. Check that all emails arrive
  3. Verify the thank you page loads
  4. Check analytics to confirm tracking works

What's Next?

Congratulations! You now have a working funnel. But the real money comes from:

  • Driving traffic (organic, paid, or social)
  • Split testing headlines and CTAs
  • Building more funnels for different offers
  • Upselling to paid plans when your list grows
