Braintree: Overview

Start Processing with Braintree

Braintree powers payments for industry leaders like Uber, Airbnb, and GitHub. Get started with Braintree and access enterprise-grade payment infrastructure.

Braintree is a full-stack payment platform acquired by PayPal in 2013. It combines the trust and reach of PayPal with a modern, developer-friendly infrastructure that scales from startups to Fortune 500 companies.

Unlike traditional payment gateways, Braintree offers a unified platform that handles credit cards, PayPal, venmo, and emerging payment methods through a single integration.

Who Is Braintree Best For?

  • High-volume e-commerce businesses
  • Subscription and SaaS companies
  • Marketplaces and platforms
  • Companies needing multi-currency support
  • Businesses requiring advanced fraud protection
  • Mobile app payment processing

Key Features

1. Unified Commerce Platform

Accept every payment type from a single integration:

  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • PayPal and PayPal Credit
  • Venmo
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • ACH Direct Debit
  • Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies (select markets)

2. Multi-Currency Support

Operate globally with support for:

  • 130+ currencies
  • Automatic currency conversion
  • Merchant settlement in preferred currency
  • Local acquiring in key markets

3. Advanced Fraud Protection

Built-in security features include:

  • Advanced Fraud Tools: Machine learning-based risk assessment
  • CVV and AVS checks: Standard verification
  • 3D Secure 2: SCA compliance for EU/UK
  • Velocity checks: Detect unusual transaction patterns
  • Customizable rules: Set your own fraud thresholds

4. Recurring Billing

Powerful subscription management:

  • Flexible billing cycles
  • Trial periods and promotions
  • Usage-based billing
  • Automatic retries for failed payments
  • Dunning management
  • Subscription management portal

5. Marketplace & Platform Payments

Split payments for marketplaces:

  • Split payments between parties
  • Escrow and hold capabilities
  • Commission calculations
  • Marketplace-specific reporting

6. Developer-Friendly API

  • SDKs for 12+ programming languages
  • Comprehensive documentation
  • Sandbox environment for testing
  • Webhooks for real-time events
  • GraphQL API (beta)

Our Verdict: Is Braintree Right for You?

When to Choose Braintree

Choose Braintree if:

  • You want PayPal as a payment option without separate integration
  • You operate in multiple currencies
  • You're building a marketplace or platform
  • You need advanced fraud protection
  • You have (or plan to have) a mobile app
  • You process high transaction volumes

When to Choose a Competitor

Choose Stripe if:

  • You need maximum customization
  • You want the best developer experience
  • You're primarily US-based with lower volume

Choose Square if:

  • You have a physical store alongside online
  • You want the simplest setup
  • You're a smaller business
