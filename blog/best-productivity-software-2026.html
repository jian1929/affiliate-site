Final Thoughts

The best productivity stack is one you'll actually use. Start with one tool that solves your biggest pain point, then expand as needed. Most teams need:

  1. A way to track tasks and projects
  2. A way to communicate in real-time
  3. A way to automate repetitive work
  4. A way to track customers and send emails

Build your stack incrementally.
