Pricing Plans (2026)

Leadpages offers straightforward pricing with two main tiers, both providing unlimited landing pages and visitors.

Plan Price Key Features
Standard $49/month Unlimited pages, unlimited visitors, 1 site, basic features
Pro $99/month Everything in Standard + A/B testing, 3 sites, advanced integrations
Advanced $199/month Everything in Pro + 5 sites, priority support, advanced analytics

Prices shown are for annual billing. Monthly billing is available at higher rates. All plans include unlimited landing pages and visitors.

What's Included at Each Tier

Standard ($49/mo): The most affordable way to get started with Leadpages. Includes unlimited landing pages and visitors, the drag-and-drop builder, basic templates, email opt-in forms, and standard integrations. Perfect for freelancers and small businesses just starting.

Pro ($99/mo): Adds A/B testing capabilities, three website domains, e-commerce functionality (including product checkout), and access to LeadDigits (SMS marketing). This is the most popular tier for growing businesses.

Advanced ($199/mo): The complete Leadpages experience with five website domains, conversion intelligence reporting, priority support, and advanced analytics. Designed for larger teams and agencies managing multiple clients.

Key Features

Drag-and-Drop Builder

Leadpages uses a straightforward drag-and-drop editor that makes page creation accessible to everyone:

  • Widget-Based Building: Drag elements like text, images, forms, and buttons
  • Section-Based Layout: Build with pre-designed sections for faster creation
  • Mobile Preview: See exactly how pages look on different devices
  • Undo/Redo: Experiment with confidence
  • Auto-Save: Never lose your work

Lead Capture Tools

True to its name, Leadpages excels at capturing leads:

  • Opt-In Forms: Embed forms directly on landing pages or site pages
  • Popup Boxes: Timed or scroll-triggered popups for lead capture
  • Alert Bars: Fixed bars for announcements and offers
  • Lead Meter: Show social proof with opt-in counters
  • Welcome Mat: Full-screen welcome gates
  • Click-To-Tweet: Viral social sharing boxes

LeadDigits (SMS Marketing)

Leadpages includes built-in SMS marketing through LeadDigits. Collect phone numbers and send text messages to subscribers. Perfect for time-sensitive offers, appointment reminders, and direct communication with leads.

E-commerce Integration

On Pro and Advanced plans, you can sell products directly through Leadpages:

  • Product Checkout Pages: Sell digital and physical products
  • Order Bump Offers: Add upsells at checkout
  • Payment Processing: Accept payments via Stripe and PayPal
  • Subscription Billing: Recurring payments for memberships or services
  • Digital Product Delivery: Automatically deliver digital downloads

A/B Testing

Available on Pro and Advanced plans, Leadpages A/B testing includes:

  • Visual Editor: Create variations without coding
  • Traffic Splitting: Control how visitors are distributed
  • Real-Time Results: Monitor performance as data comes in
  • Statistical Significance: Know when results are reliable

Template Library

Leadpages offers a curated collection of templates organized by industry and use case:

  • Lead generation
  • Webinar registration
  • Sales pages
  • Thank you pages
  • Coming soon pages

  • Webinar registration

While the library isn't as extensive as some competitors, all templates are conversion-optimized and mobile-responsive.

Integrations

Leadpages connects with popular marketing tools:

  • CRM: HubSpot, Salesforce

Website Builder

Beyond landing pages, Leadpages includes a website builder that lets you create a complete website with multiple pages, a blog, and your own navigation. This makes it a true all-in-one solution for small businesses.

Final Verdict

Is Leadpages Right for You?

Leadpages has carved out a strong position as the go-to solution for small businesses and entrepreneurs who need an affordable, all-in-one landing page and lead capture platform. The unlimited pages and visitors on all paid plans make it exceptionally good value, especially for businesses that plan to create multiple campaigns or landing pages.

Choose Leadpages if:

  • You're on a budget but need professional landing pages
  • You want unlimited pages and visitors
  • You need an all-in-one solution (pages + leads + basic sales)
  • You're a small business, coach, or consultant
  • SMS marketing (LeadDigits) appeals to you
  • You want to build a complete website, not just landing pages
  • You prefer simplicity over advanced features
  • You want e-commerce capabilities without switching platforms

Consider alternatives if:

  • You need enterprise-grade features and integrations
  • Advanced A/B testing is critical for your campaigns
  • You want the absolute most template options
  • You're an agency needing white-labeling
  • You need sophisticated post-click automation
  • Maximum customization is a priority
