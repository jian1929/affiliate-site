What Is Link Whisper?

Link Whisper is a WordPress plugin that uses artificial intelligence to automatically suggest relevant internal links as you write content. Instead of spending hours manually finding places to link to your old posts, Link Whisper does the work for you.

Internal linking is one of the most important but time-consuming aspects of SEO. Search engines like Google use internal links to understand your site's structure and distribute "link juice" throughout your content. But manually finding the right links to add to each new article can take hours.

Link Whisper solves this problem by analyzing your content and suggesting intelligent internal link opportunities in seconds.

Key Features

Automatic Link Suggestions

As you write, Link Whisper suggests relevant posts to link to based on your content. One click adds the link.

Bulk Link Building

Add multiple links to existing posts in one batch. Fix your entire site's internal linking structure in minutes.

ULL (Unlinked Mentions)

Find mentions of your brand or products that aren't linked, then convert them to backlinks automatically.

Link Report

See which posts have the most internal links and which are orphaned with no internal connections.

Click to Add

Suggested links can be added with a single click. No copy-pasting URLs or searching through your post list.

Deep Learning

The AI improves over time, learning which links perform best and suggesting increasingly relevant connections.

How It Works

Link Whisper integrates directly into the WordPress block editor (Gutenberg). As you write, a sidebar panel shows relevant internal link suggestions. Simply click to add links without leaving your writing flow.

Bulk Link Building

For existing content, Link Whisper's bulk feature scans your entire site and shows you link opportunities across all posts. You can add hundreds of internal links in minutes, significantly improving your site's link structure.

Unlinked Mentions (ULL)

Link Whisper scans your content for mentions of your brand, products, or related terms that aren't currently linked. This feature turns unlinked brand mentions into valuable internal or external links with one click.
