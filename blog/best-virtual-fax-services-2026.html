What is Online Fax?

Online fax services let you send and receive faxes via email or web interface. No fax machine, phone line, or dedicated equipment needed. Your fax becomes a PDF attachment.

Healthcare, legal, and real estate industries still rely heavily on fax for secure document transmission. Online fax combines the security of traditional fax with modern convenience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is online fax secure?

Yes! Online fax services use encrypted transmission (TLS) and storage. Enterprise plans often offer HIPAA compliance for healthcare documents.

Can I keep my existing fax number?

Yes! Most services let you transfer (port) your existing fax number. This typically takes 5-10 business days.

Do I need a phone line for online fax?

No! That's the main advantage of online fax. All faxes are sent and received via the internet.
