Common Questions

Is MailerLite really free?

Yes! MailerLite's free plan includes up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails per month. This is one of the most generous free plans in the industry.

How does the subscriber pricing work?

Pricing is based on the maximum number of subscribers you have, not how many you email. If you have 1,500 subscribers, you pay for the 2,500 subscriber plan.

Can I import my existing list?

Yes! MailerLite supports CSV imports, and can import from most major email platforms including Mailchimp, AWeber, and others.

What's the email deliverability like?

MailerLite has good deliverability rates (typically 95%+). They maintain good sender reputation and have spam check tools built in.

Does MailerLite have a mobile app?

Yes! MailerLite has iOS and Android apps for managing campaigns, viewing reports, and monitoring your email marketing on the go.
