What is Drift?Sales-Focused

Drift is a conversational marketing and sales platform designed to help B2B companies convert website visitors into qualified leads and customers through real-time, personalized conversations. Founded in 2014, Drift pioneered the concept of "conversational marketing," positioning chat as the primary channel for sales engagement rather than just customer support.

What sets Drift apart is its revenue-focused approach. While competitors like Intercom and Zendesk treat chat as part of a broader customer engagement strategy, Drift is laser-focused on using conversations to drive pipeline and revenue. The platform integrates deeply with sales processes, CRMs, and marketing automation to create seamless buyer experiences.

Key Features That Drive Revenue

1. Conversational Landing Pages

Drift's signature feature transforms static landing pages into conversational experiences. Instead of filling out forms, visitors engage in real-time conversations that qualify them and route them to the right sales rep. This approach typically captures 3-5x more leads than traditional forms.

2. AI-Powered Qualification

Drift's AI bot (Drift Intel) engages visitors 24/7, asking qualifying questions, identifying buyer intent signals, and routing high-value prospects to sales immediately. The bot learns from conversations to improve qualification accuracy over time.

3. Meeting Booking Integration

Eliminate back-and-forth scheduling with Drift's native calendar integration. Once a prospect is qualified, the bot can instantly book meetings on sales reps' calendars, reducing time-to-meeting from days to seconds.

4. Account-Based Targeting

For ABM strategies, Drift enables personalized experiences for target accounts. Surface custom messages, offers, and routing rules based on company identification, ensuring high-value prospects get VIP treatment.

5. Playbooks: Automated Conversation Flows

Build sophisticated conversation flows with Drift's visual Playbook builder. Create multi-step qualification sequences, product demonstrations, objection handling, and handoff protocols that run automatically.

6. Sales Engagement

Drift's revenue platform includes tools for outbound engagement, including email sequences, chat campaigns, and cross-channel follow-up sequences that keep prospects engaged throughout the buyer journey.

7. Pipeline & Revenue Intelligence

Track the impact of conversations on pipeline with Drift's analytics. Measure conversion rates, pipeline influenced, revenue attribution, and sales rep performance to optimize your conversational strategy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Drift and Intercom?

Drift focuses on using conversations for sales and lead generation, while Intercom takes a broader approach covering customer engagement, onboarding, and support. Drift excels at B2B pipeline acceleration; Intercom is better for customer lifecycle engagement across all stages.

Can Drift replace my CRM?

No, Drift is not a CRM. It's designed to integrate with your existing CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.) and enhance your sales process. Drift captures and qualifies leads that flow into your CRM for follow-up and management.

How does Drift's AI bot work?

Drift Intel engages visitors automatically, asking qualifying questions and routing conversations based on your Playbook configurations. The AI can identify returning visitors, match them to accounts, and surface relevant content or routing based on behavior and intent signals.

Is Drift worth the cost compared to free chat tools?

For B2B companies with meaningful traffic and sales processes, Drift's qualification and meeting booking capabilities provide ROI that free tools cannot match. However, if your goals are primarily support-focused or you have low traffic, the investment may not be justified.
