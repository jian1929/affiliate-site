Frequently Asked Questions

Are electronic signatures legally binding?

Yes. Electronic signatures are legally binding in the US under the ESIGN Act and globally under regulations like eIDAS in the EU. Major eSignature platforms maintain court-defensible audit trails that prove signature validity.

What's the difference between DocuSign and PandaDoc?

DocuSign is the industry leader with the most integrations and enterprise features. PandaDoc combines eSignature with document creation and is more affordable. For pure signatures, DocuSign excels. For proposals and quotes, PandaDoc is better.

Do I need eSignature software or is a PDF signature enough?

PDF signatures (typing your name or drawing) lack the legal protection of dedicated eSignature software. True eSignature platforms like DocuSign provide identity verification, audit trails, and court-defensible evidence of signing.

Can I use eSignature for HIPAA-compliant documents?

Yes. DocuSign, Adobe Sign, and HelloSign offer HIPAA-compliant plans for healthcare organizations. These include Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) required for HIPAA compliance.

Which eSignature is best for small business?

For small businesses, PandaDoc and HelloSign offer the best balance of features and price. SignNow is the most budget-friendly. For growing businesses, DocuSign scales best as your needs expand.
