What is Miro?

Miro is a collaborative whiteboard platform that enables teams to work together visually, regardless of location. Its infinite canvas provides unlimited space for brainstorming, planning, designing, and running workshops.

Key Features

  • Infinite Canvas: Unlimited workspace for ideas
  • Sticky Notes: Digital sticky notes in various sizes
  • Diagrams: Flowcharts, mind maps, org charts
  • Frames: Organize content into sections
  • Prototyping: Low-fi wireframes and mockups
  • Presentation Mode: Walk through your board live
  • Timer: Built-in timeboxing for workshops
  • Real-time Collaboration: See teammates' cursors and edits
