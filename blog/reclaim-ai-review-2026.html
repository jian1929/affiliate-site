What is Reclaim.ai?

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Reclaim work with Outlook?

Currently, Reclaim.ai primarily supports Google Calendar, with limited Outlook support in development.

Can teammates see my private blocks?

No, your personal habits and focus time blocks are private. Team features show only shared information.

What happens to existing meetings?

Reclaim can move existing meetings if all participants agree, but it typically protects new meetings only.

Is my calendar data secure?

Yes, Reclaim uses enterprise-grade security and never stores your calendar data longer than needed.
