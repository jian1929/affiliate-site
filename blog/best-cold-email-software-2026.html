Frequently Asked Questions

Is cold email legal?

Yes, cold email is legal when done correctly. In the US, the CAN-SPAM Act applies to cold emails. In Europe, GDPR applies. Key rules: include an unsubscribe option, use a real business address, and avoid deceptive subject lines. All the tools listed help you stay compliant.

How many emails can I send per day?

It depends on your email provider's limits and warmup status. With proper warmup through tools like Instantly.ai or Smartlead, you can typically send 50-200 emails per mailbox per day. The key is gradual scaling and maintaining good warmup practices.

What's email warmup and why do I need it?

Email warmup is the process of building sender reputation with email providers. Tools warm up your inbox by sending/receiving emails with other real accounts, teaching providers you're a legitimate sender. This dramatically improves deliverability and reduces spam folder placement.

Do I need an email finder?

Not necessarily. Platforms like Apollo.io include built-in email finding. Others like Instantly.ai and Smartlead focus purely on sending and expect you to use external tools for data. Choose based on whether you need data collection + sending, or just sending.

What's a good cold email open rate?

Industry benchmarks suggest 15-25% open rates for cold emails. Rates above 30% are excellent. Key factors: subject line quality, sender reputation, list quality, and timing. All tools listed provide analytics to track and optimize your performance.
