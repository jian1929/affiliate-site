Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best checkout page builder in 2026?

ThriveCart is our top pick for best overall value with its lifetime deal and comprehensive features. For enterprise needs, SamCart and ClickFunnels offer more advanced capabilities. For WordPress users, CartFlows provides seamless integration without leaving your existing site.

How much do checkout page builders cost?

Checkout page builders range from free (WooCommerce core) to $297/month (ClickFunnels). ThriveCart offers a unique one-time lifetime deal at $495, making it extremely cost-effective for businesses that process significant volume. SamCart starts at $49/month, while CartFlows is $129/year.

Which checkout platform has the highest conversion rates?

ThriveCart consistently achieves 6-8% higher checkout conversion rates than average through features like order bumps, 1-click upsells, trust badges, and optimized checkout flow design. ClickFunnels also performs well with its proven funnel templates and psychological triggers built into designs.

Do I need WordPress for checkout pages?

No, standalone checkout platforms like ThriveCart, SamCart, and ClickFunnels work completely independently without WordPress. They provide their own hosted checkout pages that you can link to from any sales funnel, social media, or advertising campaign. CartFlows and WooCommerce require WordPress but offer deeper integration for WordPress-based businesses.

Can I use my own payment processor?

Yes, most checkout platforms integrate with major payment processors. ThriveCart supports over 20 payment processors including Stripe, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and various regional options. WooCommerce offers the most flexibility through its extensive payment gateway plugins, including Stripe, PayPal, Square, and dozens of others.

What's the difference between checkout pages and full funnels?

Checkout pages are standalone pages designed to convert visitors into customers with minimal distraction. Full funnels include landing pages, squeeze pages, sales pages, and checkout pages working together. ClickFunnels is designed for full funnel building, while ThriveCart and SamCart focus specifically on converting checkout pages.

How do 1-click upsells work?

1-click upsells use stored payment information to offer additional products immediately after the initial purchase without requiring the customer to re-enter payment details. This dramatically increases average order value because the friction of re-entering payment is eliminated. ThriveCart and SamCart both excel at 1-click upsells.

Our Final Recommendation

For most digital product sellers and online businesses, ThriveCart delivers the best combination of features, value, and return on investment. For WordPress users who want to stay within their existing ecosystem, CartFlows integrates seamlessly with WooCommerce and adds conversion-optimized checkout flows. And for those on a tight budget willing to DIY, WooCommerce with essential plugins can create a functional checkout experience.
