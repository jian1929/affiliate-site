Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best web hosting for beginners?

Hostinger and Bluehost are excellent choices for beginners due to their user-friendly interfaces, one-click installations, and affordable pricing.

How much does web hosting cost per month?

Shared hosting typically ranges from $2-10/month, VPS hosting from $5-100/month, and dedicated servers from $100+/month. Most providers offer discounts for annual plans.

Do I need technical skills for web hosting?

No. Modern hosting providers offer intuitive control panels, one-click installers, and 24/7 support. You can set up a website in minutes without coding knowledge.

What uptime should I expect?

Aim for 99.9% uptime or higher. This translates to less than 9 hours of downtime per year. Most quality hosts offer uptime guarantees in their SLAs.

Can I switch hosting providers later?

Yes. Most providers offer free migration services. WordPress sites can be transferred easily with plugins like Duplicator or All-in-One WP Migration.
