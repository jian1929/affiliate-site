Platform Overview

Choosing between ThriveCart, ClickFunnels, and SamCart is one of the most important decisions you'll make for your online business. Each platform targets different needs and budgets. Here's what you need to know:

Key Insight

ThriveCart is a one-time payment platform with the highest affiliate commissions (50% lifetime). ClickFunnels is the all-in-one solution for building complete sales funnels. SamCart excels at conversion optimization and simplicity.

ThriveCart

Best for: Entrepreneurs who want lifetime ownership, high affiliate commissions, and a powerful checkout solution without recurring fees.

  • One-time payment of $495 (lifetime access)
  • 50% lifetime affiliate commission
  • Advanced checkout capabilities
  • No transaction fees on sales
  • Built-in affiliate management

ClickFunnels

Best for: Businesses that need complete funnel building capabilities including landing pages, membership sites, and email marketing integration.

  • Plans from $97/month
  • 40% recurring affiliate commission
  • Complete funnel builder
  • Built-in email marketing
  • Membership site builder

SamCart

Best for: Digital product sellers who prioritize conversion rates and want the simplest checkout experience possible.

  • Plans from $49/month
  • 30% recurring affiliate commission
  • Highest conversion-optimized checkout
  • Subscription management features
  • One-click upsells and bumps

For Affiliate Marketers

ThriveCart is the clear winner for affiliates. With 50% lifetime commissions on a $495 product, you earn $247.50 per sale - and the customer is linked to you forever. Compare this to ClickFunnels where you'd earn $38.80/month (even if the customer stays for years, it takes 6+ months to match ThriveCart's one-time payout).

For Your Own Business

Choose based on your needs:

  • Checkout-focused business: ThriveCart (one-time payment, no monthly fees)
  • Need complete funnels: ClickFunnels (all-in-one solution)
  • Maximum conversions: SamCart (best checkout optimization)

Quick Recommendation

If you're just starting out or want the best affiliate opportunity: Promote ThriveCart. The 50% lifetime commission with a $495 product means significant earnings per sale, and the forever cookie ensures you get credit even for delayed purchases.
