Keepa Overview

Best Overall

Keepa is the most comprehensive Amazon price tracking tool. It tracks price history, BSR (Best Seller Rank), and offers extensive filtering options.

Key Features

  • Complete Price History — See prices going back years
  • BSR Tracking — Monitor bestseller rank over time
  • Multiple Marketplaces — US, UK, DE, FR, IT, ES, CA, MX, JP
  • Deal Alerts — Get notified when prices drop
  • Product Filters — Find products meeting specific criteria
  • CSV Export — Export data for analysis
  • Amazon Coupons — View available coupons

Pricing

  • Free — Basic features, 30-day history
  • Plus — $29/month (most popular)
  • Pro — $79/month (for power users)

CamelCamelCamel Overview

CamelCamelCamel is the free option. It provides basic price tracking with a clean interface and browser extension.

Key Features

  • Free Tier — Basic tracking at no cost
  • Simple Interface — Easy to use for beginners
  • Price Drop Alerts — Email notifications when prices drop
  • Wishlist Import — Track multiple products easily
  • Browser Extension — Quick price checks while browsing

Limitations

  • Limited BSR data (only US marketplace)
  • Basic charts compared to Keepa
  • No product research features
  • Fewer marketplace options

Pricing

  • Free — Basic features
  • Premium — $5/month (more alerts, history)

Detailed Feature Comparison

1. Price History Charts

Winner: Keepa

Keepa's charts are more detailed, showing:

  • Price changes with exact timestamps
  • Buy Box price history
  • New vs. used prices
  • Comparison with other sellers
  • BSR overlaid with price

CamelCamelCamel shows basic price history but lacks the depth of Keepa's visualization.

2. Product Research

Winner: Keepa

Keepa offers powerful product research features:

  • Filter products by price, BSR, category
  • Find products below threshold prices
  • Track competitor prices over time
  • Export data for analysis

CamelCamelCamel is purely a tracking tool—no research capabilities.

3. Browser Extension

Tie

Both offer browser extensions that show:

  • Current price vs. average
  • 30/90/180-day price trends
  • Quick access to full chart
  • Price drop alerts

4. Marketplace Coverage

Winner: Keepa

Keepa covers: US, UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Canada, Mexico, Japan

CamelCamelCamel primarily covers US, with limited UK support.

5. Alert System

Tie

Both offer:

  • Price drop below threshold alerts
  • Back in stock alerts
  • Email notifications

Who Should Use What?

Use Keepa if you:

  • Are an Amazon FBA seller doing product research
  • Need BSR tracking to measure product performance
  • Sell in multiple marketplaces (UK, Germany, etc.)
  • Want to find underpriced products for retail arbitrage
  • Need detailed analytics and CSV exports

Use CamelCamelCamel if you:

  • Are primarily a buyer, not a seller
  • Want basic price tracking for personal purchases
  • Are on a tight budget
  • Only sell in the US marketplace
  • Want the simplest possible interface

Real-World Use Cases

For Amazon Sellers

Product Research: Use Keepa to analyze a product's price stability, BSR trends, and sales velocity before committing to inventory.

Competitor Monitoring: Track how competitors price your product category and adjust your repricing strategy accordingly.

Retail Arbitrage: Find products selling below their typical price to source for resale.

For Amazon Buyers

Personal Shopping: Set alerts for items you want to buy at lower prices. The $5/month premium easily pays for itself with one good deal.

Prime Day Shopping: Track prices leading up to Prime Day to see if "deals" are actually good prices.

Integration with Other Tools

Keepa integrates with popular seller tools:

  • Helium 10 — Use Keepa data alongside Helium 10's research tools
  • Jungle Scout — Combine price history with product database
  • Repricers — Inform your minimum price strategy

Conclusion

For most Amazon sellers, Keepa is the better choice. The extra cost ($29/month vs free) is justified by the comprehensive features: BSR tracking, multiple marketplaces, product research filters, and detailed analytics.

CamelCamelCamel is a solid free option for casual buyers or sellers who only need basic price tracking. But if you're serious about Amazon selling, Keepa's depth pays for itself through better product decisions.

My recommendation: Start with CamelCamelCamel's free tier to learn the basics. Once you need product research capabilities or multi-marketplace tracking, upgrade to Keepa.
