Frequently Asked Questions

What is podcast hosting and why do I need it?

Podcast hosting is a service that stores your audio files and delivers them to podcast directories like Apple Podcasts and Spotify. Unlike regular web hosting, podcast hosts are optimized for audio distribution and handle the bandwidth demands of serving large media files.

Can I switch podcast hosting platforms later?

Yes! Most podcast hosting platforms offer import/export functionality. You can move your podcast to a new host while maintaining your subscribers and ratings. Buzzsprout and Transistor both offer easy migration tools.

How much does podcast hosting cost?

Pricing ranges from free (Anchor) to $24/month for professional plans. Most quality hosts fall in the $12-19/month range. Consider your needs for storage, analytics, and monetization features when choosing.

Do I need technical skills to start a podcast?

No! Modern podcast hosting platforms like Buzzsprout and Anchor have made it incredibly easy. You can record directly in the browser, and the hosting service handles distribution to all major podcast directories.

What's the difference between podcast hosting and podcast directories?

Podcast hosting is where your audio files are stored and served from. Podcast directories (Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts) are the platforms where listeners discover and subscribe to podcasts. Your hosting service automatically distributes your podcast to these directories.

How do podcast hosting affiliate programs work?

Most podcast hosting affiliate programs offer 30% recurring commissions for each user you refer. This means you earn money every month your referrals remain paying customers. Some programs like Buzzsprout and Transistor offer some of the highest commission rates in the industry.
