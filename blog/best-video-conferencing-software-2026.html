Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free video conferencing platform?

Zoom offers the best free tier with 100 participants and 40-minute group meetings. Google Meet is excellent if you're already in the Google ecosystem. Microsoft Teams is solid for business users with Microsoft 365.

How do I choose between Zoom and Google Meet?

Choose Zoom if you need the most features and reliability. Choose Google Meet if you're heavily invested in Google Workspace or want the easiest participant experience (no app download needed).

Can I use video conferencing for webinars?

Yes. Zoom Webinars support up to 50,000 attendees. Microsoft Teams Live Events and Webex Webinars are also excellent for large audience events. For frequent webinars, dedicated platforms like Demio or EverWebinar may be better.

How do affiliate links work for video conferencing?

Is there a time limit on video calls?

Free tiers typically limit group calls to 40-60 minutes. Paid plans remove these limits. For extended meetings, Zoom Pro, Microsoft 365, and Webex paid plans offer unlimited meeting duration.

Which platform is best for large team meetings?

Microsoft Teams and Zoom Enterprise handle 1,000+ participants best. Webex is excellent for large enterprise meetings with its AI features and device management.
