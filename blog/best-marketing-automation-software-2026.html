Detailed Platform Reviews

1. HubSpot Marketing Hub

Starting at $800/month

HubSpot remains the industry leader with its all-in-one platform approach. Their comprehensive suite includes email marketing, lead scoring, CRM integration, and advanced analytics.

  • Commission: 30% recurring (HubSpot Affiliate Program)
  • Cookie Duration: 180 days
  • Pros: Full suite, excellent CRM, great support
  • Cons: Premium pricing, can be complex for beginners

2. ActiveCampaign

Starting at $49/month

ActiveCampaign combines marketing automation with CRM capabilities at an accessible price point. Perfect for growing businesses that need advanced features without enterprise costs.

  • Commission: 30% recurring
  • Cookie Duration: 90 days
  • Pros: Affordable, powerful automation, great value
  • Cons: Learning curve, limited templates

Learn More

3. Marketo

Starting at $1,200/month

Adobe Marketo is the enterprise choice for large organizations needing sophisticated lead management and account-based marketing capabilities.

  • Commission: 25% recurring
  • Cookie Duration: 90 days
  • Pros: Enterprise-grade, extensive integrations
  • Cons: High cost, steep learning curve

Learn More

4. Salesforce Pardot

Starting at $1,250/month

Pardot (now part of Salesforce Marketing Cloud) excels at B2B marketing automation with deep CRM integration and lead management.

  • Commission: 25% recurring
  • Cookie Duration: 90 days
  • Pros: Salesforce integration, B2B focused
  • Cons: Expensive, requires Salesforce

Learn More

5. Mailchimp

Starting at $59/month

Mailchimp has evolved from email marketing to a full marketing platform with automation, landing pages, and customer journey mapping.

  • Commission: 30% recurring
  • Cookie Duration: 120 days
  • Pros: Easy to use, generous free tier
  • Cons: Limited advanced features
