What is Typeform?

Typeform has redefined what online forms can be. Founded in 2012 in Barcelona, this innovative form builder pioneered the conversational form movement, transforming traditional static questionnaires into engaging, one-question-at-a-time experiences that feel more like a chat than a traditional web form.

Unlike typical form builders that present users with walls of fields to fill out, Typeform's unique approach asks one question at a time, creating a focused, distraction-free experience that naturally guides respondents through your survey or form. This seemingly simple design choice has a profound impact on completion rates, with many Typeform users reporting significantly higher completion rates compared to traditional forms.

The platform serves over 100,000 companies worldwide, from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, and has become the go-to choice for businesses that prioritize user experience and brand presentation. Typeform's elegant interface and attention to design detail make it particularly popular among marketing teams, researchers, and businesses that want forms that don't feel like forms.

Key Takeaway: Typeform is the premium choice for businesses that want forms that are as beautiful as they are functional. If user experience and brand presentation matter, Typeform delivers on both fronts.

Key Features That Make Typeform Stand Out

1. Conversational Interface

Typeform's signature feature is its one-question-at-a-time approach. Each question takes center stage on the screen, eliminating visual clutter and cognitive overload. Respondents can focus entirely on the current question, which naturally leads to more thoughtful answers and higher completion rates.

2. Beautiful Question Types

Typeform offers over 20 question types including rating scales, file upload, payment collection, NPS scores, opinion scales, and even video responses. Each type is designed with visual appeal in mind, ensuring your forms look professional without any design work on your part.

3. Logic Jumps & Branching

Create dynamic forms that adapt to respondent answers. Show or hide questions based on previous answers, send respondents down different paths, and create personalized experiences without any coding knowledge.

4. Calculator & Quiz Features

Build interactive calculators, quizzes with scoring, and lead scoring forms. The calculator feature is particularly powerful for businesses that need to generate quotes, calculate ROI, or create assessment tools.

5. Brand Customization

Every visual element can be customized to match your brand. From colors and fonts to background images and videos, Typeform ensures your forms feel like a natural extension of your brand identity.

6. Integrations

Connect Typeform with over 500 tools including Slack, Google Sheets, Mailchimp, Salesforce, HubSpot, Zapier, and more. Automated workflows can route responses to the right tools instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Typeform really worth the premium price?

For businesses where form completion rates and brand presentation matter, Typeform's premium pricing is justified. The conversational interface consistently produces higher completion rates, and the beautiful design reflects positively on your brand. However, if you need basic data collection at the lowest cost, alternatives like Google Forms are more appropriate.

Can I create quizzes with Typeform?

Yes, Typeform includes robust quiz functionality. You can create scored quizzes, provide instant feedback, and even generate lead qualification scores based on answers. The calculator feature adds even more flexibility for complex scoring logic.

Does Typeform have a free trial for paid plans?

Typeform offers a Free plan that lets you explore the platform, though with significant limitations. For full access to paid features, you can start a paid plan and get a refund within 14 days if you're not satisfied.

Can I embed Typeform on my website?

Yes, Typeform offers multiple embedding options including full embed, popover, and live embed modes. You can also share forms via direct link or create popup forms that appear on your website.

How many responses can I collect?

Response limits depend on your plan: Free (100/month), Basic (1,000/month), Plus (10,000/month), and Pro/Unlimited (unlimited). Enterprise plans have negotiated response limits.
