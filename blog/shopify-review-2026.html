For Dropshippers: Is Shopify Worth It?

Dropshippers face a unique calculation. Shopify's $29/month base cost seems high compared to free alternatives, but consider:

  • Conversion optimization - Shopify's checkout converts 30% better than custom solutions
  • Oberlo integration - Easy product sourcing and import
  • Automated fulfillment - Orders route automatically to suppliers
  • Trust factor - Customers trust Shopify-checkout stores more

If you're serious about dropshipping, Shopify is the clear choice. The monthly cost pays for itself if you convert even 1-2 extra sales per month.

Common Complaints

Hidden costs: While Shopify's base price is reasonable, many features require paid apps. Budget an extra $50-100/month for essential apps.

Transaction fees: Using third-party payment processors costs 0.5-2% extra. Use Shopify Payments (powered by Stripe) to avoid this.

Migration difficulty: Shopify's proprietary Liquid code makes migration challenging. Commit to the platform before building a complex store.

Who Should Use Shopify?

Shopify is best for:

  • Brands selling across multiple channels

Shopify may not be ideal for:

  • Content-heavy sites (use WordPress)
  • Enterprises needing full customization (use Shopify Plus)
  • Those wanting to avoid platform lock-in
