Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to sell online courses?

Platforms range from free (Thinkific, Systeme.io) to $149+/month (Kajabi). Consider transaction fees: some charge 0%, others 3-5% per sale.

Do I need technical skills to use an LMS?

No. Modern LMS platforms are designed for non-technical creators. Most offer drag-and-drop course builders and ready-made templates.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. All major platforms support custom domains. Some require paid plans for this feature.

How do I get paid?

Platforms handle payment processing and transfer funds to your bank account. Most support Stripe and PayPal.

What's the difference between course platforms and LMS?

Course platforms (Teachable, Thinkific) are for creators selling courses. LMS (LearnWorlds) often refers to platforms used by businesses for internal training.
