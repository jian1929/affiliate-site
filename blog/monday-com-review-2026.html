Pricing Plans (2026)

Monday.com offers tiered pricing based on seats, with options for teams of all sizes.

Plan Price Best For
Free Free (2 seats only) Small teams getting started
Basic $9/seat/month Teams needing core features
Standard $12/seat/month Timeline & collaboration needs
Pro $19/seat/month Advanced workflows & automation

Prices shown are per-seat, per-month when billed annually. Monthly billing is available at higher rates.

What's Included at Each Tier

Free Plan: Limited to 2 seats, 3 boards, 200+ templates. Good for trying the platform but severely limited for real work.

Basic ($9/seat/mo): Unlimited items, 5GB storage, 200+ integrations, 25 automations per month, 5 columns per board.

Standard ($12/seat/mo): Everything in Basic plus timelines, calendar view, 250 automations per month, guest access, and 250 integrations.

Pro ($19/seat/mo): Everything in Standard plus portfolios, goals, 25,000 automations per month, time tracking, and custom formulas.

Key Features

Visual Boards & Views

Monday.com excels with its visual approach to project management. You can switch between multiple view types:

  • Board View: Classic kanban-style columns and cards
  • Timeline View: Gantt-style horizontal bars for scheduling
  • Calendar View: Traditional calendar layout for deadlines
  • Map View: Geographic visualization for location-based tracking
  • Workload View: Capacity planning and resource management
  • Chart View: Visual reporting and analytics dashboards

Automation & Integrations

Monday.com offers robust automation capabilities that eliminate repetitive tasks. The automation center provides pre-built recipes for common workflows, and you can create custom automations with triggers and actions. With 200+ native integrations including Slack, Zoom, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Salesforce, and more, Monday.com connects seamlessly with your existing tools.

Collaboration Features

Real-time collaboration is baked into every aspect of Monday.com:

  • Document creation and editing with real-time sync
  • File attachments with preview capabilities
  • Comments and mentions on any item
  • Activity log tracking all changes
  • Guest access for external stakeholders
  • Notification system with email and mobile alerts

Mobile Experience

The Monday.com mobile app (iOS and Android) provides a solid experience for on-the-go work. You can view and update boards, add comments, upload files, and receive push notifications. While the mobile experience is good, some advanced features are better suited for desktop use.

Final Verdict

Is Monday.com Right for You?

Monday.com is an excellent choice for teams that value aesthetics, flexibility, and ease of use. Its colorful interface and intuitive design make it particularly appealing for creative teams, marketing agencies, and growing businesses that need a tool that team members will actually enjoy using.

Choose Monday.com if:

  • You want a visually appealing, modern interface
  • You need highly customizable workflows
  • Your team spans multiple departments or functions
  • You value automation to reduce manual work
  • You're willing to pay for premium features
  • You need strong integration capabilities

Consider alternatives if:

  • You're on a tight budget
  • You need advanced Gantt chart features
  • Your team is primarily technical/development-focused
  • You need sophisticated resource management

Overall, Monday.com earns its reputation as a top-tier project management solution. The platform strikes an excellent balance between power and usability, making it accessible to teams of all skill levels while offering the depth that growing organizations need.
