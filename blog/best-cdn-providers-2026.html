What is a CDN?

A Content Delivery Network (CDN) distributes your content across servers worldwide, reducing latency and improving load times. CDNs also provide DDoS protection, security features, and reduce bandwidth costs.

Websites using CDNs load 50-70% faster and have 40-70% lower bandwidth costs. Google gives page speed a ranking boost.
