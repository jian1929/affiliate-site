Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Elementor on multiple websites?

Yes, but only with paid plans. Each plan has a specific site limit: Essential (1 site), Advanced (25 sites), Expert (100 sites), and Studio (1,000 sites).

What happens if I exceed my site limit?

You'll need to upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional site licenses. Elementor will notify you when you're approaching your limit.

Can I cancel my Elementor subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your billing period. After that, your sites will revert to the free version.

Is there a lifetime license for Elementor?

No, Elementor only offers annual subscriptions. However, this ensures continuous updates, new features, and ongoing support.

Does Elementor offer refunds?

Elementor offers a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, you can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll pay the prorated difference. When downgrading, the change takes effect at renewal.
