What Is ClickFunnels?

ClickFunnels is the original all-in-one sales funnel builder that revolutionized how entrepreneurs sell products and services online. Founded by Russell Brunson in 2014, it has grown to over 100,000 active users who collectively generate billions in sales through their funnels.

Unlike traditional website builders that create static pages, ClickFunnels is purpose-built for conversion. Every element—from the drag-and-drop editor to the pre-built templates—is designed to guide visitors through a carefully crafted journey that maximizes sales.

Why Entrepreneurs Choose ClickFunnels

ClickFunnels eliminates the need for multiple tools. Instead of stitching together WordPress, WooCommerce, Mailchimp, and landing page plugins, you get everything in one platform. This simplicity saves hours of setup time and thousands in development costs.
