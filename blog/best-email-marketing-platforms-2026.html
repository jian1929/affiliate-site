Frequently Asked Questions

How do email marketing affiliate programs work?

When you purchase email marketing software through our links, we earn a commission (typically 30%) for the first year of your subscription. This doesn't increase your cost and helps support our research and recommendations.

Which email platform has the best deliverability?

All recommended platforms maintain excellent deliverability rates (95%+). AWeber has historically had the best inbox placement, but the differences are minimal for most users.

Can I switch email platforms later?

Yes, you can export your subscriber list and import to a new platform. Most services offer import tools and migration assistance. However, automation sequences will need to be rebuilt.

Do I need email marketing if I have social media?

Yes. Social media reach is declining due to algorithm changes. Email marketing provides direct access to your audience, higher conversion rates, and owned media you control.

How often should I send emails?

Start with consistency rather than frequency. 1-2 emails per week is ideal for most businesses. Use automation to send based on subscriber behavior rather than blast everyone at once.

What's the difference between automation and broadcasts?

Broadcasts are one-time emails sent to your full list or segments. Automations are triggered email sequences that run based on subscriber actions (sign-up, purchase, inactivity).

Which platform is easiest to learn?

MailerLite and AWeber are the easiest to learn with intuitive interfaces. GetResponse has more features but a steeper learning curve.

Can I sell products through email platforms?

ConvertKit and GetResponse offer the best e-commerce integration for selling digital products and courses. MailerLite and AWeber offer basic product selling features.

What size business is each platform best for?

  • AWeber: Small businesses, solopreneurs
  • GetResponse: SMBs, marketing teams
  • ConvertKit: Content creators, bloggers, course creators
  • MailerLite: Beginners, freelancers, small e-commerce

How important is email marketing for ROI?

Email marketing consistently delivers the highest ROI of any marketing channel. According to industry research, email generates $42 for every $1 spent, making it essential for business growth.
