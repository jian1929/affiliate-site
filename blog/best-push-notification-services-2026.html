Best Push Notification Services 2026: Complete Comparison

Push notifications are one of the most effective ways to re-engage visitors who have left your website. Unlike emails, push notifications have 50-60% open rates, and they're delivered directly to the browser without going through spam filters.

In this guide, we'll compare the top push notification services, helping you choose the right platform for your business.

Why Push Notifications Matter

Push notifications offer unique advantages:

  • Instant delivery: Messages land in seconds
  • High visibility: No spam folder to compete with
  • Cross-device reach: Works on desktop and mobile
  • No email required: Subscribers don't need to give their email
  • Cart abandonment recovery: Direct re-engagement opportunity

Best Practices for Push Notifications

  1. Ask at the right time: Don't ask immediately; let users engage first
  2. Segment your audience: Send relevant messages to specific groups
  3. Test timing: Experiment with send times for your audience
  4. Use clear CTAs: Tell users exactly what to do
  5. Don't over-notify: 3-5 notifications per week is ideal
  6. Track metrics: Monitor open rates and adjust strategy
