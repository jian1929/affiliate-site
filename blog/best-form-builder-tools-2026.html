What Google Forms lacks in bells and whistles, it makes up for in simplicity and accessibility. Anyone with a Google account can create unlimited forms, collect unlimited responses, and access basic analytics instantly. For basic data collection needs, Google Forms remains an excellent choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free form builder?

Google Forms is the best completely free option, offering unlimited forms and responses with no paid plans required. However, it has limited features compared to paid alternatives. JotForm and Typeform both offer free tiers with limited functionality that work well for basic needs.

Can I accept payments with these form builders?

Yes, most form builders support payment collection. JotForm offers the most payment gateways (25+), while Typeform, Wufoo, Paperform, and Formstack support Stripe and PayPal. Google Forms requires third-party integrations for payments.

Which form builder has the highest completion rates?

Typeform consistently achieves the highest completion rates due to its conversational, one-question-at-a-time interface. Studies show conversational forms can achieve 15-30% higher completion rates than traditional form layouts.

Do any form builders offer HIPAA compliance?

Formstack offers HIPAA compliance on Enterprise plans without requiring an additional add-on fee. Typeform offers HIPAA compliance as a paid add-on. JotForm, Wufoo, and Paperform only offer HIPAA compliance on Enterprise/.custom plans.

Can I create forms offline?

JotForm and Google Forms offer the best offline capabilities through their mobile apps. You can create forms and collect submissions without an internet connection, and data syncs when you're back online. Formstack also supports offline forms.

Which form builder is best for surveys?

Typeform excels at surveys due to its conversational approach and advanced analytics. Google Forms is excellent for free, basic surveys. JotForm offers the most template options for surveys. Formstack is best for enterprise survey needs with compliance requirements.

Do form builders offer AI features?

Typeform, JotForm, and Formstack all offer AI form generation capabilities. Typeform's AI helps create engaging conversational forms. JotForm's AI generates forms from text descriptions. Formstack's AI assists with form building and optimization.

Can I white-label forms with my own domain?

Most form builders offer white-labeling on paid plans. Formstack includes white-labeling on all plans. Typeform, JotForm, Wufoo, and Paperform offer custom domain hosting on Pro/Business plans or higher.

Which form builder has the best integrations?

JotForm and Formstack offer the most integrations with 1,000+ app connections each. Typeform offers 500+ integrations. Google Forms has the fewest native integrations but connects well with the Google ecosystem.

Can I generate PDFs from form submissions?

JotForm includes PDF generation on all paid plans with a drag-and-drop PDF editor. Formstack's Documents feature offers advanced PDF and contract generation. Typeform and Paperform also support PDF generation. Google Forms and Wufoo do not natively support PDF generation.
