Other Notable Survey Tools

Google Forms (Free)

For basic surveys and forms, Google Forms remains unbeatable value. It's completely free, integrates with Google Sheets, and offers enough customization for simple surveys. The limitations: basic analytics and limited design options.

Zoho Survey ($19/month)

Part of the Zoho ecosystem, Zoho Survey offers solid survey features with excellent Zoho integration. Best for businesses already using Zoho apps. The free plan supports up to 10 responses per form.

QuestionPro ($49/month)

QuestionPro bridges the gap between basic survey tools and enterprise platforms. Offers advanced features like conjoint analysis, max diff, and enterprise survey programming at more accessible prices than Qualtrics.

Hotjar ($32/month)

While primarily a behavior analytics tool, Hotjar includes survey capabilities that pair perfectly with session recordings and heatmaps. Ideal for UX research and website optimization feedback.
