Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free time tracking software?

Clockify offers the best completely free plan with unlimited users and projects. Toggl and Harvest also offer generous free tiers, but Clockify's is the most comprehensive for teams on a budget.

Can time tracking software help with productivity?

Yes! Tools like RescueTime automatically track your computer usage and reveal time-wasting habits. All time trackers help you understand where your hours go, enabling better time management and accountability.

Do time trackers have mobile apps?

Yes, all major time tracking software offers mobile apps for iOS and Android. Toggl, Harvest, Clockify, and Hubstaff all have fully-featured mobile apps for tracking time on the go.

Can I integrate time tracking with other tools?

Most time tracking software integrates with popular tools. Toggl integrates with 100+ apps including Asana, Trello, and Slack. Harvest integrates with QuickBooks, Stripe, and accounting software. Hubstaff integrates with project management and payroll tools.

Which time tracking software has the best invoicing?

Harvest has the best built-in invoicing features, allowing you to create invoices directly from tracked time. It integrates with accounting software like QuickBooks and Xero for seamless billing workflows.

Is employee time tracking legal?

Yes, employee time tracking is legal in most jurisdictions. However, transparency with employees about what is being tracked and how the data is used is essential. Hubstaff offers configurable monitoring options to balance oversight with privacy.
