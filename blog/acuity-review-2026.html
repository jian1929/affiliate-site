What is Acuity Scheduling?

Acuity Scheduling is an appointment scheduling platform specifically designed for service-based businesses. Founded in 2006 and now part of Squarespace, it has become the go-to choice for wellness professionals, beauty salons, fitness studios, health practitioners, and anyone who offers time-based services.

Unlike general-purpose scheduling tools, Acuity was built from the ground up for businesses that need to manage classes, packages, intake forms, and client payments. Its deep integration with Squarespace makes it particularly attractive to businesses already using that platform.

Bottom Line: Acuity Scheduling is the ideal choice for service-based businesses that need class scheduling, package management, and intake forms. Its specialized features make it more powerful than general schedulers for these use cases.
