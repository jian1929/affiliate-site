Can I switch newsletter platforms later?

Yes, all major platforms offer migration tools or services. However, it's best to choose carefully upfront to avoid subscriber fatigue from re-subscription campaigns.

Do I need a large audience to make money with newsletters?

No. Even with 500-1,000 engaged subscribers, you can generate affiliate income and potentially $500-2,000/month with premium subscriptions or affiliate links. Quality engagement matters more than quantity.
