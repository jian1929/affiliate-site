What is SamCart?

SamCart is a dedicated checkout page and cart optimization platform that has been helping digital product sellers, course creators, coaches, and online entrepreneurs increase their conversions since around 2014. Founded in the United States, SamCart has grown to serve over 10,000+ businesses, making it one of the most popular choices for anyone serious about optimizing their online sales process.

Unlike comprehensive all-in-one platforms like ClickFunnels that attempt to handle everything from landing pages to email marketing, SamCart takes a focused approach. The platform specifically targets the checkout experience—the moment when a potential customer decides whether to complete their purchase or abandon their cart. This singular focus has allowed SamCart to develop some of the most sophisticated conversion optimization features in the e-commerce space, including one-click upsells, intelligent order bumps, and built-in A/B testing capabilities that most competitors charge extra for or don't offer at all.

For affiliate marketers and digital product sellers, the checkout page is arguably the most critical element of your entire sales funnel. You can drive all the traffic you want through carefully crafted landing pages and compelling sales copy, but if your checkout experience is clunky, slow, or prone to abandonment, you're leaving significant revenue on the table. SamCart addresses this challenge head-on by providing a platform specifically engineered to reduce friction and maximize the percentage of visitors who complete their purchases.

Key Features of SamCart

SamCart's feature set is impressive and well-thought-out, with every tool designed specifically to increase your conversion rates. Here's a comprehensive look at what the platform offers:

One-Click Upsells and Downsells

One of SamCart's standout features is its sophisticated upsell and downsell system. With a single click, customers can add complementary products to their order without re-entering payment information. The platform supports both pre-checkout upsells (shown before payment) and post-checkout upsells (shown after the initial purchase). This feature alone has helped many merchants increase their average order value by 20-50% without any additional traffic investment.

Order Bumps

Order bumps allow you to offer add-on products with a simple checkbox on your checkout page. Think of it like the candy displays at a supermarket checkout counter—customers can add relevant items with one click while they're already in buying mode. SamCart makes this incredibly easy to implement and test, allowing you to optimize which bumps perform best with different audiences.

Drag-and-Drop Page Builder

SamCart includes a visual page builder that lets you create beautiful, conversion-optimized checkout pages without any coding knowledge. The builder comes with over 50 professionally designed templates, and you can customize everything from colors and fonts to images and product layouts. The builder is intuitive enough for beginners while offering enough depth for experienced marketers who want fine-grained control over their pages.

Built-in A/B Testing

Unlike many platforms that charge premium prices for split testing capabilities, SamCart includes A/B testing functionality in its Growth and Scale plans. You can test different headlines, pricing displays, checkout layouts, and even entire page templates. The platform automatically calculates statistical significance, so you know when you have a winner. This feature alone can be worth hundreds of dollars per month in additional revenue from optimized conversion rates.

Subscription Saver Features

For businesses offering recurring subscriptions or payment plans, SamCart includes powerful tools to reduce churn. The subscription saver automatically attempts to save failing subscriptions by sending targeted emails and offering customers alternative payment options before their subscription lapses. This feature can significantly improve your long-term customer retention and lifetime value.

Coupon and Discount Management

SamCart makes it easy to create and manage discount codes, limited-time offers, and promotional campaigns. You can set percentage or dollar amount discounts, create usage limits, set expiration dates, and even create tiered pricing structures. The platform also supports affiliate-exclusive coupons, making it easy to run partner programs without additional software.

Multi-Currency Support

In today's global marketplace, being able to sell in multiple currencies is essential. SamCart supports over 100 currencies, automatically detecting customer location and displaying prices in their local currency. This feature reduces friction for international customers who might otherwise abandon a purchase due to unfamiliar currency confusion.

Exit Intent Capture

SamCart can detect when a visitor is about to leave your checkout page and display a targeted popup or offer. This exit-intent technology gives you one last chance to capture the sale, whether through a discount offer, a compelling reason to complete the purchase, or an email capture for follow-up. Many merchants report significant conversion improvements from well-timed exit-intent campaigns.

Affiliate Management

For merchants who want to leverage affiliate marketing, SamCart includes built-in tools to manage your affiliate program. You can recruit affiliates, provide them with unique tracking links and promo codes, automatically calculate and pay commissions, and track affiliate performance—all within the same platform where you manage your products and checkout pages.

Detailed Analytics and Reporting

Understanding your numbers is critical for optimization. SamCart provides comprehensive analytics that show you exactly which products are selling, which checkout pages convert best, where customers are dropping off, and how your upsells and order bumps are performing. The reporting dashboard makes it easy to identify opportunities for improvement and track the impact of your optimization efforts.

Pros and Cons

Like any platform, SamCart has its strengths and weaknesses. Here's an honest assessment to help you decide if it's the right choice for your business.

Pros

  • Conversion-focused design: Every feature in SamCart is engineered to increase conversions, from one-click checkout to intelligent upsell placement. If conversion optimization is your priority, SamCart delivers.
  • Excellent page builder: The drag-and-drop editor is intuitive and produces professional-looking checkout pages without requiring design or coding skills. With 50+ templates, you have plenty of starting points.
  • Powerful upsell capabilities: The one-click upsell and order bump features are among the best in the industry, with sophisticated logic for pre and post-purchase offers.
  • Built-in A/B testing: Split testing is included in Growth and Scale plans, allowing you to continuously optimize your pages without additional tools or costs.
  • No transaction fees: Unlike some competitors, SamCart doesn't take a percentage of your sales, making it cost-effective as your revenue grows.
  • Subscription saver: For recurring billing businesses, this churn-reduction tool can significantly improve customer lifetime value.
  • Strong affiliate management: Built-in affiliate tools mean you don't need separate software to run an affiliate program.
  • Multi-currency support: Selling globally is straightforward with automatic currency detection and display.
  • 30-day money-back guarantee: Plenty of time to test the platform and ensure it's right for your business.

Cons

  • No free plan: Unlike some competitors, SamCart doesn't offer a permanent free tier. However, the 7-day trial and 30-day money-back guarantee mitigate this concern.
  • Limited landing page features: While SamCart excels at checkout pages, its landing page capabilities are more limited compared to all-in-one funnel builders like ClickFunnels. You'll need separate tools for blog posts, complex landing pages, or membership sites.
  • Learning curve for advanced features: While the basics are intuitive, fully leveraging features like A/B testing, subscription saver logic, and advanced integrations requires some learning time.
  • Limited third-party integrations: SamCart connects with major email marketing platforms and payment processors, but its integration library isn't as extensive as some competitors. Check that your essential tools are supported before signing up.
  • Monthly costs can add up: At $59-199 per month, costs accumulate over time, especially if you need multiple admin seats or custom domains across different businesses.
  • Not ideal for physical products: SamCart is optimized for digital products, courses, and services. If you primarily sell physical goods, you may find other platforms more suitable.

ClickFunnels is an all-in-one platform that handles everything from landing pages to email marketing to checkout. This makes it a better choice if you want a single tool for your entire funnel.
