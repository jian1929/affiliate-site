Pro-Exclusive Features That Matter

1. Theme Builder

Design every part of your WordPress theme without code:

  • Custom headers and footers
  • Archive pages (blog, categories, tags)
  • Single post and page templates
  • 404 error page
  • Search results page

Why Theme Builder Matters

Without Theme Builder, you're limited to page-level customization. With it, you can create a fully custom WordPress theme using only Elementor's visual editor. This alone saves hundreds to thousands in developer fees.

2. Popup Builder

Create high-converting popups with advanced targeting:

  • Exit-intent popups
  • Time-delayed popups
  • Scroll-triggered popups
  • User-role-based popups
  • Inline embedded popups

3. WooCommerce Builder

Customize your entire e-commerce store:

  • Product archive pages
  • Single product pages
  • Cart and checkout pages
  • My Account page
  • Product Add to Cart widget

4. Form Builder

Create professional forms without plugins:

  • Contact forms
  • Lead capture forms
  • Newsletter signup forms
  • Multi-step forms
  • File upload forms

5. Additional Pro Widgets (50+)

  • Price Table - Display pricing tiers
  • Price Box - Highlight featured plans
  • Testimonial Carousel - Customer reviews slider
  • Posts Carousel - Blog posts grid/slider
  • Portfolio - Gallery layouts
  • Slides - Hero image sliders
  • Video - Embed with custom player
  • Facebook Buttons - Social login

Who Should Upgrade to Pro?

Upgrade If You...

  • Build client websites (Theme Builder alone is worth it)
  • Run a WooCommerce store
  • Need popups for lead capture or exit intent
  • Want to create custom form layouts
  • Manage multiple WordPress sites
  • Need custom post types and archive templates

Stay on Free If You...

  • Only need basic pages (no complex layouts)
  • Use a theme you don't want to replace
  • Are a hobbyist blogger
  • Just started with WordPress

Calculating Your ROI

For Freelancers & Agencies

If you build client websites, Pro pays for itself with one project:

  • Developer rates for custom theme: $2,000-5,000
  • Elementor Pro Essential: $59/year
  • You save: $1,941-4,941 per client site

For E-commerce Stores

WooCommerce Builder alone justifies the upgrade:

  • Custom WooCommerce development: $1,000-3,000
  • Elementor Pro + WooCommerce Builder: $59/year
  • Plus: Better conversions from custom product pages

For Content Marketers

Consider Pro if you need:

  • Custom archive templates for better SEO
  • Popup forms for email capture
  • Post-related widgets (carousel, grid)

Our Verdict

Upgrade to Pro

Yes, for most users. The Essential plan at $59/year offers exceptional value. The Theme Builder alone can save hundreds in development costs. If you run any kind of business website, Pro is worth it.

The free version is excellent for learning and simple projects. But once you need custom headers, popups, WooCommerce customization, or form builder, Pro becomes essential.

Money-Saving Tip

Wait for Elementor's frequent sales (often 20-40% off). Black Friday and Cyber Monday typically offer the best discounts. Even without a sale, the Essential plan at $59/year pays for itself quickly.
