Frequently Asked Questions

How do website security affiliate programs work?

When you purchase website security services through our links, we may earn a commission. This doesn't increase your cost and helps support our research and recommendations.

Do I need both CDN and security service?

For most sites, Cloudflare's free tier provides both. If you need malware removal and more advanced security, add Wordfence (free) or Sucuri (paid).

Can Cloudflare protect against malware?

Cloudflare focuses on network-level protection (DDoS, WAF, bots) but doesn't scan for malware on your server. For malware scanning, use Wordfence or Sucuri alongside Cloudflare.

What's the difference between WAF and firewall?

A WAF (Web Application Firewall) specifically protects web applications by filtering HTTP traffic. Traditional firewalls filter network traffic. Most security services use WAFs.

Is free security enough?

For low-traffic personal blogs, Cloudflare's free tier + Wordfence free plugin provides adequate protection. Business and e-commerce sites need more comprehensive solutions.

Can I use multiple security services?

Yes, many users combine Cloudflare (network security + CDN) with Wordfence or Sucuri (server-level protection). However, avoid conflicting rules.

How important is DDoS protection?

If your site has ever experienced or is likely to experience DDoS attacks, protection is essential. Cloudflare and Sucuri offer excellent DDoS mitigation.

What about WordPress security?

WordPress powers 40% of all websites, making it a top target. Use Wordfence or Sucuri specifically for WordPress protection, plus follow security best practices.

Do security services slow down my site?

Cloudflare's CDN often speeds up sites despite security processing. SiteLock and Wordfence may add slight overhead, but modern implementations minimize impact.

What's included in malware removal?

Sucuri's unlimited removal means they clean your site as many times as needed. SiteLock includes removal on higher tiers. Wordfence helps identify issues but requires manual cleanup.
