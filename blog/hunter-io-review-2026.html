What Is Hunter.io?

Hunter.io is an email finding and verification platform that helps professionals find and verify business email addresses. Founded in 2015, Hunter has grown to become one of the most popular tools for B2B outreach, sales prospecting, and recruitment.

The platform allows you to search for email addresses by company domain, find specific people by name and company, verify email deliverability, and build targeted prospect lists—all without leaving your browser.

With over 2 million users and a reputation for accuracy, Hunter.io is particularly popular among:

  • Sales teams and SDRs
  • Recruiters and HR professionals
  • Marketers running cold email campaigns
  • Business development professionals
  • Journalists and researchers

Key Features

Email Finder

The core feature that made Hunter famous:

  • Domain Search: Find all email addresses associated with a company domain
  • Email Finder: Enter a person's name and company to find their email
  • Bulk lookups: Process multiple domains or names at once
  • Confidence score: Shows how confident Hunter is in each email
  • Sources: See where Hunter found each email

Email Verifier

Ensure your emails actually reach the inbox:

  • Syntax check: Validate email format
  • MX record check: Verify the domain accepts emails
  • SMTP verification: Confirm the mailbox exists
  • Disposable email detection: Flag temporary email addresses
  • Free verifier: Verify single emails for free

Campaigns (Cold Email)

Built-in outreach functionality:

  • CSV import: Upload prospect lists
  • Email templates: Create personalized templates
  • Sequences: Set up automated follow-up sequences
  • Scheduling: Send at optimal times
  • Tracking: Monitor opens, clicks, and replies
  • Warm-up: Improve deliverability with email warm-up

Domain Search

Find all professional emails at a company:

  • Company profiles: Detailed info on each company
  • Employee count: See company size
  • Social patterns: See common email formats (e.g., john.doe@company.com)
  • LinkedIn integration: Connect with prospects on LinkedIn

Integrations

  • Chrome extension: Find emails while browsing
  • Salesforce: Sync leads automatically
  • HubSpot: CRM integration
  • Zapier: Connect to 3,000+ apps
  • API access: Build custom integrations
