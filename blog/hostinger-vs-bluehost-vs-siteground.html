Platform Overview

Choosing the right web hosting provider is one of the most important decisions for your online business. Hostinger, Bluehost, and SiteGround are three of the most popular options, each with distinct strengths and weaknesses.

Key Insight

Hostinger offers the best value with prices starting at $2.99/month and 60% affiliate commissions. Bluehost is WordPress-recommended and great for beginners. SiteGround excels in performance and customer support but at premium prices.

Hostinger

Best for: Budget-conscious users who want premium features at the lowest prices.

  • Prices starting at $2.99/month
  • 60% affiliate commission
  • LiteSpeed servers with NVMe storage
  • Custom hPanel (easier than cPanel)
  • Global data centers
  • Free SSL and CDN

Bluehost

Best for: WordPress users who want official WordPress recommendation and easy setup.

  • Prices starting at $2.95/month
  • 30-65% affiliate commission
  • Official WordPress recommended host
  • One-click WordPress installation
  • Free domain for first year
  • 24/7 phone and chat support

SiteGround

Best for: Businesses that prioritize performance, security, and premium support.

  • Prices starting at $3.99/month
  • 40-60% affiliate commission
  • Google Cloud infrastructure
  • Managed WordPress hosting
  • Daily backups included
  • Staging environments
