Key Features That Make FreshBooks Shine

1. Gorgeous Invoice Design

FreshBooks invoices look professional and polished, helping you make a great impression on clients. Choose from a variety of templates, customize colors and fonts, add your logo, and create reusable product and service line items. The automatic late payment reminders feature helps ensure you get paid on time without awkward follow-ups.

2. Effortless Time Tracking

Time is money for service professionals, and FreshBooks makes it easy to track every billable hour. Use the desktop timer or mobile app to capture time as you work, then seamlessly convert tracked hours into client invoices. This feature alone can significantly improve your revenue by ensuring you actually bill for all the time you spend on client work.

3. Smart Expense Management

Connect your bank accounts and credit cards to automatically import and categorize expenses. FreshBooks uses AI to suggest categories and can even detect recurring expenses. Receipt photos can be captured directly from the mobile app, eliminating the need to hoard paper receipts.

4. Project and Milestone Tracking

The Plus and Premium plans include project management features that let you track progress against milestones, monitor project profitability, and collaborate with team members. This is particularly valuable for consultants, designers, and developers managing multiple client projects simultaneously.

5. Client Management

Keep all client information organized in one place. FreshBooks stores contact details, communication history, and project information, making it easy to pick up where you left off with any client. The client portal feature allows clients to view invoices, pay online, and access project documents.

6. Estimates and Proposals

Create professional estimates and proposals to win new business. Convert accepted estimates to invoices with a single click, streamlining your workflow from proposal to payment.

7. Mobile Accounting

The FreshBooks mobile app is among the best in the industry, allowing you to manage your finances from anywhere. Track time, capture receipts, send invoices, and check on your business health from your phone or tablet.

8. Financial Reports

Generate profit and loss statements, expense reports, invoice summaries, and more with a few clicks. These reports give you the financial visibility you need to make smart business decisions and simplify tax preparation.

How Does FreshBooks Compare to Alternatives?

FreshBooks vs QuickBooks

QuickBooks is the more comprehensive solution with better scalability and deeper accounting features. It's better suited for businesses with employees, inventory needs, or complex accounting requirements. FreshBooks wins on simplicity and is more cost-effective for pure service businesses with straightforward needs. If you're a freelancer or small agency, FreshBooks often provides better value. If you're growing into a more complex business, QuickBooks scales more seamlessly.

FreshBooks vs Xero

Xero offers unlimited users on all plans, making it more collaborative, and has stronger accounting features. However, FreshBooks provides a more intuitive experience for non-accountants. Xero is better for businesses that need sophisticated accounting workflows, while FreshBooks excels at being approachable and easy to use daily.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FreshBooks good for freelancers?

FreshBooks is arguably the best accounting software for freelancers. Its intuitive interface, beautiful invoices, and excellent time tracking are specifically designed for service-based solo professionals. The Lite plan at $19/month offers tremendous value for freelancers who primarily need to invoice clients and track expenses.

Can I accept online payments with FreshBooks?

Yes. FreshBooks integrates with major payment processors including Stripe, PayPal, and BluePay. You can accept credit cards, ACH bank transfers, and other payment methods. Clients can pay invoices directly through the FreshBooks client portal or embedded payment links, making it easy for them to pay promptly.

How does FreshBooks handle taxes?

FreshBooks organizes your expenses by category throughout the year, making tax preparation much easier. The system generates profit and loss reports and can export data for use with tax software or your accountant. However, FreshBooks doesn't file taxes directly. It focuses on tracking and organizing your financial data to simplify the tax process.

Can I use FreshBooks with my accountant?

Absolutely. FreshBooks offers an Accountant Hub designed specifically for accounting professionals. Your accountant can access your books, run reports, and make adjustments without needing a separate FreshBooks login. This collaborative feature makes it easy to work with tax professionals and bookkeepers.

What happens if I exceed my client limit?

If you exceed the client limit on your plan, FreshBooks will prompt you to upgrade to the next tier. The Plus plan supports 25 clients, while Premium offers unlimited clients. This scaling ensures you can continue using FreshBooks as your business grows without losing your historical data.

Is FreshBooks secure?

FreshBooks takes security seriously, using 256-bit SSL encryption, secure data centers, and regular security audits. The platform supports two-factor authentication for added account protection. As a public company with millions of users, FreshBooks maintains rigorous security standards to protect your financial data.
