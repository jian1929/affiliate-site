When the Free Plan is PERFECT

The free plan genuinely works if you:

  • Are starting out - Build your first funnel and email list without spending money
  • Have under 2,000 contacts - Plenty for testing and early audience building
  • Send under 1,000 emails/month - Enough for weekly newsletters
  • Don't need webinars - Skip the $47/month Webinar plan
  • Don't need custom domain - Use the systeme.io subdomain

When You MUST Upgrade

Upgrade to Startup ($27/mo) when:

  • You hit 2,000 contacts
  • You need more automation workflows
  • You want a custom domain (yourdomain.com instead of yoursite.systeme.io)
  • Your team grows beyond 1 person

Upgrade to Webinar ($47/mo) when:

  • You want to run live or automated webinars
  • You need evergreen webinar funnels
  • Webinar marketing is part of your strategy

Upgrade to Unlimited ($97/mo) when:

  • You have 25,000+ contacts
  • You need unlimited team members
  • You're running a serious agency or business
