What Is Elementor?

Elementor is the world's leading WordPress page builder, powering over 10 million websites. It allows anyone to create stunning, professional websites without writing a single line of code. Whether you're a freelancer building client sites, an agency handling multiple projects, or a business owner managing your own web presence, Elementor provides the tools you need.

Founded in 2016, Elementor has revolutionized WordPress website building with its intuitive drag-and-drop interface. The platform offers both a free version and premium plans, with the Pro version unlocking advanced features like theme builder, popups, WooCommerce integration, and more.

Why Elementor Dominates WordPress

Elementor commands over 40% market share among WordPress page builders. Its success stems from continuous innovation, a thriving ecosystem of templates and add-ons, and a community of millions of users. When someone needs to build a WordPress site, Elementor is often the first recommendation.

The Editing Experience

Elementor's editor is where it truly shines. Unlike traditional WordPress editing where you switch between editor and preview, Elementor shows your changes in real-time. You can drag widgets from a sidebar panel, drop them onto your canvas, and arrange them however you like.

The interface is clean and intuitive. Each element has extensive styling options accessible through a sidebar panel. You can adjust typography, colors, spacing, borders, and shadows without touching code.
