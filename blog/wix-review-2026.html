What is Wix?

Wix is one of the most popular website builders in the world, empowering over 200 million users to create stunning websites without any coding knowledge. With its drag-and-drop editor and hundreds of templates, Wix makes professional web design accessible to everyone.

Who Should Use Wix?

  • Small Business Owners needing a professional online presence
  • Freelancers & Creatives showcasing portfolios
  • Bloggers wanting an easy-to-use publishing platform
  • Entrepreneurs testing business ideas quickly

Key Features That Set Wix Apart

Drag-and-Drop Editor

Wix's intuitive editor lets you place elements anywhere on your page. No coding required — just drag, drop, and customize. It's the most flexible editor in the website builder space.

800+ Designer Templates

Choose from hundreds of professionally designed templates organized by industry. Restaurant? Portfolio? Online store? Wix has a template for that.

Wix ADI (Artificial Design Intelligence)

Answer a few questions and Wix ADI creates a custom website for you in minutes. Perfect for those who want a great-looking site without design skills.

App Market

Extend your site's functionality with 250+ apps. Add booking systems, live chat, email marketing, social feeds, and more — all from the Wix App Market.

Wix eCommerce

Sell products both online and in-person with Wix Stores. Manage inventory, process orders, accept multiple payment methods, and track shipping — all built-in.

SEO Tools

Built-in SEO tools help you rank higher in search engines. Custom meta tags, clean URLs, structured data support, and SEO recommendations.
