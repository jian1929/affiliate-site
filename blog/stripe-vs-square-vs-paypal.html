Pricing Breakdown: Stripe vs Square vs PayPal

Stripe Pricing

Stripe offers transparent, volume-based pricing:

  • Online transactions: 2.9% + $0.30 per successful card charge
  • In-person transactions: 2.7% + $0 per card present
  • ACH transfers: 0.8% (max $5)
  • Wire transfers: $15 outgoing
  • Currency conversion: 1% above base rate
  • No monthly fees, setup fees, or hidden costs

Square Pricing

Square offers competitive rates with no monthly fees:

  • Online transactions: 2.6% + $0.10
  • In-person transactions: 2.6% + $0
  • Manually entered: 3.5% + $0.15
  • Square for Retail: 2.5% + $0 with hardware
  • Square for Restaurants: 2.6% + $0
  • No monthly fees

PayPal Pricing

PayPal offers various pricing tiers:

  • Online transactions: 3.49% + $0.49 per transaction
  • In-person (QR code): 2.99% + $0
  • Micropayments: 5% + $0.05 (under $10)
  • International: Additional 1.5% currency conversion
  • PayPal Here: 2.7% for card swipes
  • No monthly fees for basic accounts

Features Comparison

Stripe Features

Stripe is known for its developer-first approach and comprehensive features:

  • Stripe Elements: Pre-built, customizable UI components
  • Stripe Radar: Machine learning fraud protection
  • Stripe Connect: Marketplace and platform payments
  • Stripe Billing: Subscription and invoicing management
  • Atlas: Company incorporation services
  • Terminal: POS hardware for in-person payments
  • Sigma: Built-in business intelligence
  • Global reach: 135+ currencies, 46+ countries

Square Features

Square provides an all-in-one ecosystem for businesses:

  • Square POS: Full-featured point of sale system
  • Square for Restaurants: Kitchen display, table management
  • Square for Retail: Inventory tracking, barcode scanning
  • Square Appointments: Scheduling and booking
  • Square Online: Free e-commerce website builder
  • Square Payroll: Full-service payroll processing
  • Square Capital: Business loans based on sales
  • Gift cards: Digital and physical gift card programs

PayPal Features

PayPal offers extensive features and brand recognition:

  • PayPal Checkout: One-tap purchasing experience
  • PayPal Credit: Buy now, pay later options
  • Venmo: Social payment app integration
  • PayPal Business: Invoice, expenses, and more
  • PayPal.me: Simple payment links
  • Braintree: Enterprise payment gateway
  • Global reach: 200+ countries, 25+ currencies
  • Brand trust: 400+ million active accounts

Integrations & APIs

Stripe Integrations

Stripe leads in developer experience:

  • E-commerce: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce
  • Subscriptions: Stripe Billing, Recurly, Chargebee
  • Accounting: QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, Wave
  • APIs: REST API, Stripe.js, Mobile SDKs
  • Webhooks: Real-time event notifications

Square Integrations

Square excels in POS ecosystem integration:

  • E-commerce: Square Online, Weebly, Ecwid, BigCommerce
  • Food ordering: DoorDash, Uber Eats integration
  • Accounting: QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks
  • CRM: HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp

PayPal Integrations

PayPal offers broad integration support:

  • E-commerce: Most major platforms support PayPal
  • Marketplaces: eBay integration (owned)
  • POS: PayPal Zettle, QR code payments
  • Enterprise: Braintree gateway for large businesses

Our Verdict: Which Payment Processor Wins?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use multiple payment processors?

Yes, many businesses use multiple processors. Common combinations include Stripe for online + Square for POS.

Which processor has the lowest fees?

Square has the lowest in-person rates (2.6%). Stripe has the best developer features. PayPal generally has the highest fees.

Do these processors offer business loans?

Square Capital offers loans based on your Square sales history. Stripe and PayPal have limited lending options.

Conclusion

Your choice between Stripe, Square, and PayPal depends on your business type:

  • Online/SaaS businesses: Choose Stripe
  • Retail/restaurant businesses: Choose Square
  • International selling: Consider PayPal or Stripe
  • Developer-focused businesses: Choose Stripe
