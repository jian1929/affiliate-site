E-Commerce Integration

Klaviyo dominates e-commerce with native integrations for Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Magento. Automated flows for abandoned cart, post-purchase, welcome series, and win-back campaigns come pre-built.

Mailchimp offers e-commerce features but requires paid plans. Product recommendations and abandoned cart are available on Standard+.

ActiveCampaign relies on integrations for e-commerce but offers powerful automation once connected.

ConvertKit and AWeber are not built for e-commerce and lack native product/cart functionality.

Best Platform by Use Case

E-Commerce Businesses (Shopify, WooCommerce)

Winner: Klaviyo

Klaviyo's free plan (250 contacts, 500 emails/month) is exceptional for small e-commerce stores. Native Shopify integration means abandoned cart emails work out of the box. The ROI typically pays for itself within the first campaign. Growth plan at $45/month handles most growing stores.

Bloggers and Content Creators

Winner: ConvertKit

ConvertKit is purpose-built for creators. Landing pages, opt-in forms, and email sequences designed for building audiences. Creator plan ($9/month) includes everything needed. The visual automation builder makes complex sequences simple. Creator Pro ($29/month) adds newsletter referrals—great for growth.

Small Businesses Needing Simplicity

Winner: Mailchimp or AWeber

Mailchimp's free plan is excellent for businesses under 500 contacts. The interface is intuitive, templates are professional, and basic automation covers most needs. Upgrade to Essentials for A/B testing and custom branding.

Advanced Marketers Needing Power

Winner: ActiveCampaign

ActiveCampaign's automation capabilities rival enterprise platforms at mid-market prices. Site tracking, CRM integration, and machine learning-powered predictions help sophisticated marketers achieve results. Plus plan ($49/month) unlocks most business features.

Free Plan Deep Analysis

Mailchimp Free: 500 contacts and 1,000 emails/month. Excellent for very small lists. Templates are professional, and basic automation is included. The catch? Charges increase rapidly as your list grows.

Klaviyo Free: 250 contacts and 500 emails/month for e-commerce. If you're on Shopify, this is the obvious starting point. The e-commerce automation flows alone justify using Klaviyo over competitors.

ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, AWeber: No free plans. All offer free trials (14-30 days) but require payment from day one.

Migration Considerations

If you're switching platforms, consider migration complexity:

  • Mailchimp to anywhere: Export is straightforward, but automation won't transfer
  • ConvertKit: Good export options, creator-focused data structures
  • ActiveCampaign: Complex migrations due to advanced automation
  • Klaviyo: Native Shopify migration tool, excellent support

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Choosing based on price alone: A $10/month platform that doesn't integrate with your stack costs more in the long run
  • Ignoring deliverability: All platforms here have good deliverability, but Klaviyo and ActiveCampaign excel
  • Overbuying features: Most small businesses don't need ActiveCampaign's enterprise features
  • Underestimating list growth: Plan for where you'll be in 12 months, not just today

Money-Saving Tips

  • Clean your list regularly: Lower contact counts = lower costs
  • Annual billing: Save 20-30% across all platforms
  • Start free: Mailchimp or Klaviyo free plans let you test thoroughly
  • Use free trials strategically: 14-30 days is enough to build key automations
  • Negate annually: Most platforms offer discounts when asked
