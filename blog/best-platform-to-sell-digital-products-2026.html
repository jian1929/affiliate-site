How to Choose the Right Platform

1. What are you selling?

  • Just templates/ebooks: Gumroad or Lemon Squeezy
  • Courses + memberships: Podia or Kajabi
  • Software licenses: Lemon Squeezy
  • Physical + digital: Shopify

2. What's your budget?

  • $0 to start: Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy
  • Can pay monthly: Podia, Shopify, Kajabi
  • No transaction fees: Podia, Systeme.io

3. Do you need tax handling?

  • Selling globally: Lemon Squeezy (automatic)
  • US only: Any platform works
  • Want to avoid: Manual tax calculation

4. What's your technical skill?

  • No code experience: Gumroad, Podia
  • Some technical: Lemon Squeezy
  • Need customization: Shopify (or custom)

Use Case Recommendations

You're selling Notion templates

→ Gumroad or Lemon Squeezy

Both have license key features and simple checkout. Gumroad if you want the simplest setup; Lemon Squeezy if you want automatic tax handling for global customers.

You're creating online courses

→ Podia or Kajabi

Podia is more affordable; Kajabi has more advanced features. Both include course hosting, payments, and email tools.

You're selling SaaS software

→ Lemon Squeezy

Built specifically for software sales with license key management, subscription billing, and global tax compliance.

You want an e-commerce store

→ Shopify

The industry leader for selling products online. Best for serious e-commerce businesses with multiple product types.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Choosing based on price alone - The cheapest platform isn't always best. Consider features and fit.
  2. Ignoring transaction fees - A platform with no monthly fee might have higher transaction fees that eat into profits.
  3. Not considering future needs - Start with what you need, but check if the platform can grow with you.
  4. Overlooking tax compliance - If you sell globally, automatic tax handling saves hours of headache.

Platform Migration

Worried about getting locked in? Most platforms allow data export. However, migrating customers and products takes time. Choose carefully upfront.
