What Is Loom?

Loom is a video messaging platform that allows you to record your screen, camera, or both simultaneously. Unlike traditional video recording tools, Loom is built specifically for async communication - meaning your recipients can watch your videos on their own schedule, without requiring real-time interaction.

Originally designed for remote teams and businesses, Loom has evolved into a versatile tool used by content creators, educators, customer success teams, and increasingly, affiliate marketers who want to create personalized video content at scale.

Who Should Use Loom for Affiliate Marketing?

Loom is particularly valuable for affiliate marketers who:

  • Create product review videos or tutorials
  • Need to communicate personally with affiliate managers
  • Want to create video walkthroughs for their audiences
  • Produce training content for their affiliate links
  • Need quick screen recordings for troubleshooting or demonstrations
  • Prefer async communication over live meetings

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Loom for YouTube affiliate reviews?

Yes! Many affiliate marketers use Loom to record product reviews and tutorials. The Business plan removes Loom branding so your content looks professional.

Does Loom work on mobile devices?

Yes, Loom has iOS and Android apps for recording on the go. However, the full editing features are best accessed via desktop.

Is Loom owned by Atlassian?

Yes, Atlassian acquired Loom in 2023. This brought integration benefits but some feature changes occurred during the transition.

How is Loom different from Zoom recording?

Loom is async-first - designed for one-way communication where viewers watch on their own time. Zoom is synchronous, built for live meetings.
