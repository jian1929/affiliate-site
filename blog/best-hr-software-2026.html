Frequently Asked Questions

What is HR software?

HR software (Human Resource Information System or HRIS) is a digital platform that helps businesses manage their workforce. This includes payroll processing, benefits administration, employee records, time tracking, recruitment, onboarding, performance management, and compliance.

How much does HR software cost?

HR software pricing varies widely. Small business options like Gusto and BambooHR start around $6-7 per employee per month plus base fees. Enterprise solutions like Workday and ADP require custom quotes and can cost significantly more, often with implementation fees.

Do I need payroll software or full HR software?

If you only need payroll processing, standalone payroll software may be sufficient. However, full HR software offers benefits like integrated time tracking, benefits management, and employee self-service that save time and reduce errors. Consider your current and future needs.

Can HR software help with compliance?

Yes, HR software helps maintain compliance by tracking employee documents, managing required certifications, handling tax filings correctly, and maintaining audit trails. Enterprise solutions like ADP and Workday have extensive compliance features for complex regulatory requirements.

What features should small businesses look for in HR software?

Small businesses should prioritize ease of use, employee self-service, automatic payroll tax filing, benefits administration, and onboarding tools. Look for software that grows with you and doesn't require extensive IT resources to manage.

How long does it take to implement HR software?

Implementation time varies by solution. Cloud-based HR software like Gusto can be set up in days to weeks. Enterprise solutions like Workday may take months and require dedicated implementation teams. Plan for data migration, integration testing, and employee training.
