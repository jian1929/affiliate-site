Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free email marketing service?

Sendinblue offers the best free plan with unlimited contacts and 300 emails per day. Mailchimp and AWeber also offer solid free tiers limited by contact count. ConvertKit's free plan is limited to 300 subscribers.

How much does email marketing cost?

Pricing varies by provider and list size. Most charge based on contacts, ranging from $0 for basic free plans to $100+ monthly for 10,000+ contacts. Sendinblue offers unlimited contacts with volume-based sending limits.

Can I switch email marketing platforms?

Yes, most platforms offer migration assistance. Export your subscribers as a CSV file and import to your new platform. However, your automation sequences and custom configurations won't transfer.

What is a good email open rate?

Industry averages range from 15-25%. Factors affecting open rates include subject line quality, sender reputation, list quality, and email timing. ConvertKit users often report higher open rates due to their creator-focused audience.

Do email marketing services offer automation?

All major platforms offer automation. ConvertKit excels with visual automation builders. GetResponse offers the most comprehensive automation including webinars. Even free tiers typically include basic welcome sequences.
