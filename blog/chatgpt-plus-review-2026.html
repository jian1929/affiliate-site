Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use ChatGPT Plus for commercial affiliate content?

Yes, OpenAI allows commercial use of ChatGPT-generated content. However, you should edit and add your unique perspective to ensure content quality and avoid potential issues with search engines.

What's the difference between GPT-4 and GPT-3.5?

GPT-4 is significantly more capable at complex reasoning, follows instructions better, has more factual accuracy, and produces more nuanced and contextually appropriate responses. For affiliate marketing tasks like writing persuasive copy, GPT-4 noticeably outperforms GPT-3.5.

Does ChatGPT Plus have usage limits?

ChatGPT Plus has higher limits than the free tier, but limits can still apply during periods of very high demand. For most affiliate marketing tasks, the limits are more than sufficient.

Can I use DALL-E 3 images for my affiliate website?

Yes, images generated with DALL-E 3 are generally available for commercial use. However, verify the current OpenAI usage policies and consider that highly generic AI images may not perform as well for SEO as original photography.

How does ChatGPT Plus compare to Jasper for affiliate marketing?

ChatGPT Plus is more versatile and costs less ($20 vs $49+/month for Jasper). However, Jasper offers pre-built marketing templates, brand voice consistency, and SEO integrations that ChatGPT doesn't have out of the box.
