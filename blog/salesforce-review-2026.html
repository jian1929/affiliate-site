In 2026, Salesforce offers more products than ever, but complexity and cost remain major considerations. ## What is Salesforce? Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform built primarily for enterprise sales, service, and marketing teams. Unlike HubSpot's unified approach, Salesforce operates as an ecosystem of specialized "clouds": - **Sales Cloud** – Pipeline and deal management - **Service Cloud** – Customer support and case management - **Marketing Cloud** – Email, advertising, and journey orchestration - **Commerce Cloud** – B2B and B2C e-commerce - **Experience Cloud** – Portal and community building - **Tableau** – Analytics and business intelligence - **Slack** – Internal communication (acquired 2021) ## Salesforce Pricing 2026 ### Sales Cloud | Edition | Price/User/Mo | Key Features | |||--| | Starter | $25 | Basic pipeline, accounts, contacts | | Professional | $80 | Full pipeline, forecasts, collaborative forecasts | | Enterprise | $165 | Advanced automation, web-to-lead, territory management | | Unlimited | $330 | Everything + 24/7 support, migration services | | Einstein 1 | $500 | AI-powered, industry-specific solutions | ### Service Cloud | Edition | Price/User/Mo | Key Features | |||--| | Growth | $25 | Basic case management, email case creation | | Professional | $75 | Full case management, email-to-case, Macros | | Enterprise | $150 | Omnichannel routing, AI-powered macros | | Unlimited | $300 | Advanced analytics, Premier Success Plan | ### Additional Costs to Consider -
