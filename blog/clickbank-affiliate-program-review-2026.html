What is ClickBank?

ClickBank is one of the world's largest affiliate marketplaces, specializing in digital products. Founded in 1998, it has paid out over $7 billion in commissions to affiliates worldwide. Unlike traditional affiliate networks, ClickBank focuses primarily on digital products like online courses, ebooks, software, and membership sites.

Key Takeaway

ClickBank offers some of the highest commission rates in affiliate marketing (50-75%), with instant approval for new affiliates. Perfect for content creators in the digital product space.

Getting Started with ClickBank

  1. Create Your ClickBank Account

    Visit ClickBank's affiliate signup page and complete the registration form. You'll need to provide basic information and agree to the terms of service.

  2. Browse the Marketplace

    Use ClickBank's marketplace to find products that align with your audience. Filter by category, commission rate, gravity score (sales performance), and payout type.

  3. Generate Your HopLink

    Once you find a product, ClickBank generates your unique HopLink (their term for affiliate link). This tracks all sales from your promotions.

  4. Create Content

    Write reviews, comparisons, tutorials, or integration guides. Place your HopLink naturally within valuable content that helps your audience.

  5. Drive Traffic

    Promote your content through SEO, social media, email marketing, or paid ads. Monitor your ClickBank dashboard for performance.

  6. Get Paid

    ClickBank pays weekly via direct deposit, PayPal, or wire transfer. Minimum payout is $10 for direct deposit or $100 for wire.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does ClickBank pay for referrals?

Yes, ClickBank has a two-tier program where you can earn 5% of first-tier sales for referring other affiliates.

What is the ClickBank gravity score?

Gravity indicates how many affiliates have made sales for a product in the past 12 weeks. Higher gravity means proven sales performance.

Can I promote ClickBank products on YouTube?

Yes, YouTube is an approved traffic source. Include your HopLink in video descriptions and create review or tutorial content.

What payment methods does ClickBank offer?

Direct deposit, PayPal, wire transfer, and check. Minimum payouts vary by method ($10-$100).

Are ClickBank products high quality?

ClickBank vets vendors, but product quality varies. Look for products with high gravity, positive reviews, and professional sales pages.
