Unlimited ($7/user/mo): Everything in Free plus unlimited storage, unlimited history retention, custom reporting, goals, folders, calendars, and Gantt charts.

Business ($12/user/mo): Everything in Unlimited plus custom roles, workload management, time tracking by user, resource management, whiteboard, client billing, and priority support.

Enterprise: Everything in Business plus advanced permissions, white labeling, custom contracts, dedicated success manager, and SSO/SAML.

Key Features

Views for Every Working Style

ClickUp offers more view options than virtually any competitor:

  • List View: Classic hierarchical task list with nested subtasks
  • Board View: Kanban-style columns for workflow management
  • Box View: Visual sprint board with workload capacity
  • Gantt View: Timeline-based project scheduling
  • Calendar View: Traditional calendar with deadline management
  • Mind Map View: Visual brainstorming and workflow planning
  • Whiteboard: Collaborative canvas for ideation and process design
  • Activity View: Real-time team activity stream

Document Creation & Wikis

ClickUp Docs provides a Notion-like experience for creating rich-text documents, wikis, and knowledge bases. You can embed tasks, link documents to projects, and create interconnected knowledge systems that keep your team aligned.

Automation & Templates

ClickUp's automation engine handles repetitive tasks efficiently:

  • Pre-built automation recipes for common workflows
  • Custom automation builder with triggers and actions
  • 300+ actions per workspace on Unlimited+ plans
  • Automation runs tracked and logged
  • Extensive template library for various use cases

Goal Tracking & Reporting

ClickUp's Goals feature allows organizations to align team efforts:

  • Company-wide, team, and individual goals
  • Key Results for measuring goal progress
  • Custom dashboards for real-time insights
  • Burndown charts and progress visualizations
  • Automated reporting delivery

Native Time Tracking

Unlike many competitors that require paid add-ons, ClickUp includes comprehensive time tracking across all plans:

  • Built-in timer with start/stop functionality
  • Manual time entry
  • Time estimates and remaining time tracking
  • Timesheets and time reports
  • Integrations with payroll systems
