Final Recommendation

The Verdict

All three platforms are excellent choices for their respective use cases. There's no universally "best" scheduling tool - only the best tool for your specific needs.

Our Recommendations

Best Overall: Calendly dominates with its combination of ease of use, extensive integrations, and industry-leading brand. If you're unsure which to choose, Calendly is the safest bet that won't disappoint.

Best for Service Businesses: Acuity Scheduling offers specialized features (class scheduling, packages, intake forms) that general schedulers can't match. If you run a salon, spa, or wellness center, Acuity is purpose-built for you.

Best for Enterprise: OnceHub provides advanced workflow automation and routing that simpler tools lack. If you need lead qualification and complex booking workflows, OnceHub delivers.

Quick Decision Guide

  • General meetings and calls = Calendly
  • Beauty/wellness services = Acuity
  • Sales team qualification = OnceHub
  • Maximum integrations = Calendly
  • Squarespace users = Acuity
  • Budget-conscious = Calendly (free tier)
  • Enterprise security = OnceHub
  • Ease of use = Calendly
