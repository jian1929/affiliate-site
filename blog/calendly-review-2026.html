What is Calendly?

Calendly is a scheduling automation platform that has revolutionized how professionals and businesses manage appointments. Founded in 2013, it has grown to become the industry standard for scheduling, serving millions of users across startups, agencies, and Fortune 500 companies.

At its core, Calendly eliminates the back-and-forth email exchange that traditionally accompanies scheduling meetings. Instead of sending emails proposing times and waiting for responses, you share a link that shows your real-time availability. Invitees choose a slot that works for them, and the meeting is automatically booked, confirmed, and added to calendars on both ends.
